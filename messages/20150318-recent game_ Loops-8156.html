A very simple game that you could play with Xs and Os. It's clearly related to Go but has much simpler rules, and while obviously not as "deep", it can still produce some interesting competition.

Beginners will probably just want to play with one move per turn and only their own side's pieces. Also, some players might just prefer a small komi to the pie-rule.

The rules are explained here: https://www.scribd.com/doc/255619096/Loops

Here is the primary reason the idea interests me: http://boardgamegeek.com/thread/1286815/variants

It's perfect for the IGGC sandbox, and here are some grids you can print:
http://boardgamegeek.com/thread/1337170/print-boards
