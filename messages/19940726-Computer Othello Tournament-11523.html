            THE 2nd PADERBORN COMPUTER-OTHELLO TOURNAMENT

                         October 1-2, 1994
                        Paderborn (Germany)



The 2nd Paderborn Computer-Othello tournament will take place at the 
University of Paderborn (Germany) October 1-2, 1994 (a weekend).

We will provide Sun-Sparcstations (Sun-OS 4.1.3, X11R5) - so, if your
program is portable you don't have to bring your own hardware.
The tournament will be played on the Internet Othello Server (IOS).
In order to make things simple (for us) every UNIX-program must use
the client-module to connect to IOS directly. You can get the C-source- 
code via e-mail typing

              telnet faust.uni-paderborn.de 5000
              user-id
              [password]
              mail Files/client.h [your-e-mail-address]
              mail Files/client.c [your-e-mail-address]

and you can test your program intensively there.
 
For those PC-programmers who are not able to come to Paderborn, we will 
provide *few* 486 PCs ranging from 33MHz Laptops to 50MHz towers. 
Please note: first come, first served. Apart from that, any hardware is 
allowed as long as the operator is able to carry it. (Please don't tell 
us that you can carry your CM5 or 1024-Transputer-System ;-)

The entry fee is DM 70,- to be paid at the registration desk or via postal
mail - *only* cash (DM) is accepted. It will be used to buy some nice
prizes for the best programs and to provide the programmers with cold 
drinks etc. Accomodation is available at a price of some DM 70,-
(per person & night, including breakfast).

The tournament modus and the timetable will be announced when the exact 
number of entries is known.
