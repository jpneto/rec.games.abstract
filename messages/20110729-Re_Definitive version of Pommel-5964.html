Hi Luigi

The captain capture is limited to the first empty square, no long
slide after the last captured piece.  Without this, and combined with
linear capture, I think captains would have been too deadly and the
game becomes a race to be the first to promote and then use the first
captain to decimate the opposition, a pattern I wanted to avoid.

You are correct about the existence of cooperative drawn positions,
and I realize now that I should have seen this (wipes egg off face).
I believed that one side would always be able force a win, but it is
also possible that neither player will see the forcing win and the
game will not terminate.  It also now occurs to me that there can be
positions, albeit rare, in which there is not a forced win.  I retract
my assessment of Pommel as a drawless game until such time as I revise
the rules (if I ever do so).  Christian, if you read this, please
correct the Mindsports description.

Thanks for your astute observations!
