"Life is fundamentally futile and great games are a tribute to
precisely that." - an interesting quote I found on Christian's website
(http://mindsports.nl/).  It's funny because in the days before I read
that I had been pondering the importance of abstract games, if any.

to the point.  Games are a tribute to the futile.
