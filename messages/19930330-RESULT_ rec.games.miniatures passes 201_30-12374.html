TALLY OF VOTES FOR PROPOSED NEWSGROUP "REC.GAMES.MINIATURES"

Unofficially rec.games.miniatures has passed by 204 yes votes to 30 no
votes. 3 "yes" votes are suspect and are likely to be dropped, providing an
amended total of 201 yes to 30 no votes.

I happily present for your approval and verification a tally of votes taken 
for the "rec.games.miniatures" proposal.

Voting closed on the 28th of March and I would like to thank all who took
the time to vote and especially those people who provided morale sustaining 
feedback via mail during the RFD and the CFV; you know who you are - drop me
a line sometime and I'll give you a few reasons why you should (and should
not!) run a newsgroup proposal.

Subject to the considerations of the voting process, I look forward to
contributing with you to what looks like a promising newsgroup.

The vote was taken by Jonathan Kamens, the moderator of news.answers,
without whom this newsgroup would not have been possible. Thanks.


VOTING IRREGULARITIES

Please take the time to check your vote and read the result requirements
below. If there are irregularities which may invalidate the vote please post
to news.announce.newgroups and notify me ASAP.

Two votes stand out as clear duplicates, from the enthusiastic but naughty
Frank Gilson and Jason Peter Mackinlay - they can only be JUNTA players!

A third vote looks a little suspect: it's from ftpmail@holguin.cu, I'll let
the moderator of n.a.newgroups rule on this but for the moment this vote is
invalid. [[ news.announce.newgroups moderator's note:  if it could be
verified through that address, it would be fine.  Regardless it is
irrelevant; the proposal still passes.  -- tale ]]

I therefore present an amended provisional tally of 201 yes votes to 30 no
votes which is a nice healthy pass in anyone's language.
