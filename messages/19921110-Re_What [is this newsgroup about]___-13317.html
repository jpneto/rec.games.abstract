Rec.games.abstract is for games of pure strategy which have no existing
newsgroup.  Pure strategy implies no hidden knowledge and no element of
chance.  Thus, by way of example, Othello and Pente are two that satisfy
both criteria.  Chess and Go do as well, but they have existing groups.

Btw, I speak only for myself, and if I'm in error, don't say I didn't 
warn you...
