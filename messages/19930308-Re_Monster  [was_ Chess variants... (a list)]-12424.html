Yup.  We are talking about very different games.  Allowing black the
power to advance the pawns changes everything.  After further study of
the variant where black pawns cannot advance (except through capture),
I am pretty sure this is a win for white, assuming that white can manage
the rook+rook+queen mate at the end (which seems easy to mess up, but
forceable with perfect play)
