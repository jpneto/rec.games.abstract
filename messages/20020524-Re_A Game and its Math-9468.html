As I understand, the American game (8x8 board) of checkers has been
solved already quite some time ago, and there are quite some additional
rules for championships.  The European (10x10 board) version is (sa far

playing differences.

played on 10 and 8 - square boards. English-US draughts uses an 8x8 board
(the game of checkers is not a different game - it's just the US name for
English-US draughts).
