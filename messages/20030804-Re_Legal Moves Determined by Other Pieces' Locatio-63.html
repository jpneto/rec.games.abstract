 i just received a game from the flea market world
 called Trippples[tm].  this game came out in 1972 and
 features a changeable board over which 2 pieces move.


 each piece (a circle or a square) moves according to
 the board tile trhat the other piece is sitting on.
 so your legal moves are determined by *where* the
 other piece is.


 there are 8 ways to leave a board square and each piece
 has either a blank (4) or a graph symbol showing three
 ways to leave the square (56) and then the start & finish
 corners (4).
