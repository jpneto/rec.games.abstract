An 8x24 abstract strategy wargame:
https://cf.geekdo-images.com/images/pic1389159_md.png
Pieces:
6 Leaders - may activate themselves or any other single piece within 2 squares.
 Leaders step 1or 2 squares, and may change direction between the 1st & 2nd step.
16 Men - step 1 square in any direction. 
4 Cannon - leap diagonally 1, 2, or 3 squares, cannot change direction in a move.
4 Heavy Cavalry - leap orthogonally 1, 2, or 3 squares, cannot change direction.
4 Light Cavalry - move like chess knights, leaping over other pieces.

Players get 4 activations/turn. No piece may move without being activated that turn.

Capture by landing directly on an opposing piece with a legal move which ends in the square of capture.

Win by capturing all the opposing Leaders.
