Subject: New MSG Game: Shuffle
From: Mark Steere <marksteere@gmail.com>
Date: 28/11/2012, 03:05
Newsgroups: rec.games.abstract

8x8 checkerboard.  24 red singletons in first 3 rows.  24 white singletons opposite.  The goal is to bear all of your checkers off the far end of the board.  Draws can't happen.

No stacks.  No passing.  The two players, Red and White, only move checkers of their own color, one move per turn.  Red goes first.

Checkers move and kill as Chess queens, but only in the directly and diagonally forward directions.
