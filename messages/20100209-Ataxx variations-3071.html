Has anyone ever tried any of the following?

  * 3-D (interior cubes have 26 neighbors)
  * 3x3x3 with middle cube unavailable (otherwise it's a trivial
      first-player win if it's initially empty, or else a trivial
      one-cube bonus to the player who initially occupies it)
  * 4x4x4 or larger with all cubes available

  * 3-D, alternating cubes (interior cubes have 8 neighbors)

  * Outer surface of a cube (NxN x 6)
  * Outer surface of a tetrahedron, octahedron, or icosahedron
      (NxN x 4, 8, or 20)

  * Cylinder (NxN with one pair of edges connected)
  * Mobius strip (NxN with one pair of edges twist-connected)
  * Torus (NxN with both pairs of edges connected)
  * Klein bottle (NxN with both pairs edges connected, one twisted)
  * Projective plane (NxN with both pairs of edges twist-connected)
