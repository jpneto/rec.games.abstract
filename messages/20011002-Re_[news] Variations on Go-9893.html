DENSITY

Mutator Density(N,M) is a rule stating that for each NxN cell (N odd) inside
the board should have at most M pieces on it. With no restrictions, i.e.,
Density(N,N*N) we get standard Go. I estimate that M > N*sqrt(N) to create
an interesting game. In the example, the game has Density(3,5), so White can
protect is soldier by playing at [2] for example, since Black cannot play
[1] or else the density would be 6 on that 3x3 square.
