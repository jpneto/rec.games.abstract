Based on the success of last year's shogi workshop we are excited to
announce the

                  SECOND ANNUAL EUROPEAN SHOGI WORKSHOP

                    1st, 2nd and 3rd  of October 1993
             EMBL, Meyerhofstr. 1, 69012 Heidelberg, Germany

                            Sponsored by
                                SETA
                              of Japan

 * Learn to play and win at Shogi from Europe's top players.
 * Sample traditional Japanese cuisine.
 * Test your skills against the computer.
 * Play in a tournament and win a trophy from Japan.


WORKSHOP SCHEDULE

Day 1 Friday evening, 1st October:  Shogi Videos and informal game
playing.

Day 2 Saturday 9:30-19:00, 2nd October: The Shogi Workshop
Several short formal lectures will cover basic through advanced theory,
A traditional Japanese lunch will be followed by many small, interactive
workshops.

Day 3 Sunday 9:30-16:00, 3rd October: Shogi Tournament
Quick play tournament with Shogi trophies and prizes from Japan.

Note: The language of the workshop is English.

Accommodation (Heidelberg Jugendhereberge) meals and entry fee is 100DM.
Entry only (including the Japanese lunch) is 35DM

Register early and enjoy a 50% refund!!
