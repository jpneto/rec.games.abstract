A very interesting source for analyzing games is Richard's PBeM Archive:

http://northumbria.tripod.com/pbemarchive.html

I compared Andantino to The Game of Susan. I took 40 games (no forfeited
games, no premature resigns when there was not yet a winning sequence of
moves in sight) and started to count:


GAME      | 1rst Player | 2nd Player |
--------------------------------------
Andantino | 92.5%       | 7.5%       |
--------------------------------------
Susan     | 45.0%       | 55.0%      |
-------------------------------------

winner in %



This is a very strong hint that Andantino is a sure win for the first
player, while Susan has a slight second move advantage (like International
Morris).
