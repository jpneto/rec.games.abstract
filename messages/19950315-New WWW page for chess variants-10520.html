I started recently with a WWW home page for chess variants, i.e., games
that are different from chess, but related to chess, like Japanese Chess
(Shogi), Chinese Chess, historic variants like Shatranj, 3-dimensional chess,
bughouse, etc. Presently, there are rules for some of these, and several
pointers to other places.

Additions are welcome.

The page is:

http://www.cs.ruu.nl/~hansb/d.chessvar/index.html
