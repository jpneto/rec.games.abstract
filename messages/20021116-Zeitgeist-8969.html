Just wanted to let everyone know about a new game on the web.  It's
called Zeitgiest and it's free to play. What is Zeitgeist? Well,
simply, it's an internet based game in which players pit 5 famous
personalities (real or fiction) against another players 5 famous
personalities with the outcome determined by the number of internet
search results garnered by each personality.  If anyone is interested,
you can visit the site at:
http://gameday.de/zeitgeist

or e-mail me at: zeitgeist@gameday.de
