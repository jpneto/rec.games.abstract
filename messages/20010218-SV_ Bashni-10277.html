There is complete rules, and some strategy in Abstract Games magazine,
http://www.abstractgamesmagazine.com for subscription information.

As I recally, capture is by short leap for checkers, long leap for kings,
and flying king rule. A king needs to play as to be able to continue the
capturing sequence if possible, but maximum jump is not forced.
