: Here is a little puzzle.

: You have 10 stacks of 10 coins.  One whole stack of coins is counterfeit.

a more interesting puzzle:

you have a scale which, when an equal # of coins are placed on
  each side, will tilt to the left, tilt to the right, or stay balanced.

how, in three weighings, can you determine which is the off-weight coin?
