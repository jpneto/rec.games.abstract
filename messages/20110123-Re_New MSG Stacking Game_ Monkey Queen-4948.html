Basically he didn't generate enough babies when he had the chance and got
overwhelmed and checkmated.  There's kind of a "phase 1" in Monkey Queen
where you just try to lay out as many babies as you can.  At any time during
that phase though you can go for a checkmate, and more queens do not always
win.  Still, going for a checkmate without having enough babies to back it
up is a common beginner error.
