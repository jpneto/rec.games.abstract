Ok I had a game at iggc with Mark [ and won btw ;) ].  Not that anyone
else needs a nutshelling of the rules, but I rather like them short...

Rive

Don't touch the largest group on the board unless you're forced to.
If you can place a stone onto the board not adjacent to any other
stone then you must do so.

Otherwise...
Place a stone onto the board that doesn't merge any groups.
or
Place a stone on the board which does merge groups, reduce the size of
the new group to one stone larger than the largest of the merged
groups without splitting it, and move again.
