  With all the talk about Hex lately, I thought I'd post a
  hex puzzle that occured in a recent email game of mine:

                     A B C D E F G H I J K

       	            - - - - - - H H H - -   1
                   - - - - - - - v v H v   2
                  - - v H H H H v H H v   3
                 - - - v v v v - v H v   4
                - - H - v H - - - - v   5
               - - - - - v - - H - -   6
              - - - - - - - - - - -   7
             - - - - - - - - - - -   8
            - - - - - - - - - - -   9
           - - - - - - - - - - -   10
          - - - - - - - - - - -   11

  H to play and win (H=horizontal, v=vertical)
  Enjoy!
