Wanted:

    Plateau computer player program.

Plateau is a new abstract strategy game for two players.  It is 
available for sale in select stores around the world.  A computer
version has been developed that facilitates play by two human
players via email.  The email version (called Honor Plateau) needs
a basic computer player facility so that novices can get up to
speed.

Extensive work has been done developing heuristics.  Also, lots of
the basics such as rule checking and board generation are complete.

We at Magnolia Games Inc. have a very liberal policy concerning
computer implementations of Plateau.  For complete information
on the game and to get a copy of Honor Plateau contact one of
addresses below.
