It is the year 3006.

A few years ago, five people entered the Kamir ascension coffins in the
black tower of Nocturn and emerged forever changed.

Now, a clan of Shohobian Mystics who ventured beyond the multiverse nexus
have returned to Nocturn, restored the fallen black tower, and proclaimed
themselves the new Kamir.

Their leader, formerly known as Eye Mordecai, has begun contacting those
whose minds were last touched by the Kamir in normalspace.

The old gods are dead. It is time for the new gods to arise.
