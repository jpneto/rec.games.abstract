Gipf is a game with potetials. Each potential _can be_ linked to a game.
Zertz is one of these potentials in gipf, and Zertz is one of these games.
So Zertz does not have potentials.



"Potential" is an English word that has nothing to do with Gipf!
It means: a possibility to do something.

For instance:

Go groups which have the potential to form two eyes (even if it's your
opponent's turn now)  are unconditionally "alive".
