Is it true that Pente is the same as Go-Moku?

 
No. Pente was ripped off from Renju, not Go Moku. There is no capturing of
stones in Go Moku. If there are any rule differences between Renju and the
plagerized Pente, they are minor, sort of like if you 'invented' a game just
like chess, but instead of the 50 move rule, you had a 55 move rule. I think
it's embarrassing that they could get such a ripoff onto the market and
ridiculous that it's sold so many millions of dollars worth. Well, I gotta
get back to work on my novel, Police Action and Truce.
