Online since 1998, OtherSpace is an original-theme online roleplaying game
that takes place in a 31st Century galaxy where mankind dwells among aliens
and travels faster-than-light around the cosmos in an evolving storyline.

Our primary focus is on roleplaying - acting out scenes involving multiple
players - from small chance meetings to massive world-changing events.

Save yourself, your friends or the universe itself! Explore the galaxy. Make
a name for yourself as a superstar roleplayer.

We're always looking for new players to bring our world to life.

The game is free to play.

The Wiki at http://oswiki.jointhesaga.com is where we're building up a vast
amount of information about the game universe and its associated coded
systems, as well as logs of roleplaying activities.
