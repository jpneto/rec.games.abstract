You don't need every atom in the universe - the question is how long it
takes to traverse the tree, not store the results. After all, you can put a
marker at any node where there's a forced win or draw below it, and free up
the subtree for use in the rest of the problem.

Of course, this would mean the storage requirements when you were done would
be very simple - a mark in the top node saying either "White wins", "Black
wins", or "Draw."

I'd characterize the amount of time needed to do the traversal as "too
damned long."
