Another interesting game, Mark, making the most of minimal equipment,
and it should be a game with great clarity, which as I have said
before is a quality I value highly.

I think I would drop the distinction between stalemate and win by
depriving of moves.  The distinction is not all that clear, and if
you're going to award a player a win for leaving his opponent with no
legal move, you might as well apply that rule to all situations,
including "stalemate".  I think having fewer rules is clearer, unless
you have a specific reason for awarding a draw by stalemate.
