Many people have written rules for this game.  Most include:

(1)  Rules are analogous to tennis, as is scoring.
(2)  Fouls are:
(2a)  Statements
(2b)  Non Sequiturs
(2c)  Rhetoric (i.e., a question not needing an answer)
(2d)  Repetitions
(2e)  Synonyms

Some people don't use rule 2e.

For an interesting twist, play the game of "Non Sequiturs" where each
statement has to be a non sequitur.  The last person to keep a straight
face wins.  (I invented this.)
