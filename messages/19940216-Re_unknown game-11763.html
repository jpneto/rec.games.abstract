In article <CL9EF7.DFr@cs.utwente.nl> sijben@cs.utwente.nl (Paul Sijben) writes:

[a board with 15 holes in a triangle arrangement, and 14 pegs in 4 colors]


My guess: triangular peg solitaire, and the peg colors can be ignored.
A move consists of jumping one peg over an adjacent one into a hole on the
other side, and removing the jumped peg (thus X X o -> o o X).  The object
is to get from the initial configuration (one empty hole) to its opposite
(one peg left, in the same hole that was initially empty).
