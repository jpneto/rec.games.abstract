What if you had three types of stones: plain stones, stones with an X
on them, and stones with a dot on them.

Each stone could claim liberties in only one plane:

Plain stones in the normal go breadth and width;
X stones in the plane containing breadth and heighth;
Dot stones in the plane containing width and heighth.

I imagine it would be too complicated to grasp, but it would solve the
multiple-liberty problem, yes?
