David Bush writes
] 
] I disagree. If getting your king to the 8th rank before your opponent does the
] same would win the game, most of the endgames you refer to would turn into a
] king race. The pawns would lose their importance.

Not true.  In king & pawn endgames, the primary object is to penetrate to the  
opponent's side of the board with the king, in order to pick off the pawns, which  
almost always are blocked by their counterparts.  The only effect of this  
modification would be to increase the value of the opposition unduly.
