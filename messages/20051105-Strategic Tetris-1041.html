Dear all,

we have just created our first shareware game in Java and LWJGL.

Its a multiplayer Tetris where two players play on the same board at
the same time. It's not just about being fast. You have to co-operate
and co-ordinate with your friend in order to go higher! Hitting each
others pieces makes them move and rotate so its not as easy as it may
sound! :)

There are also online highscore tables for the registered users where
they can compare themselves to other people from all over the world!

Visit http://donut.spitaki.org and go to the games section to read
more about it and download the demo!
