No comment on it, but it is available commercially in a package of six
games called _The Best of Dragon Games_ from TSR.  It was published in
Dragon #128 (Dec, 87), for those who just want to hunt up the back
issue and find the rules.  It's called _King's Table_, which I believe
is a translation of Hneftafl - not sure.
