Hi all,

Letting you know that the Android app for BoxOff has been updated yet again:

https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.cyningstan.boxoff

There is now a statistics page so players can track their progress.  The app is still completely free, with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Although BoxOff is a solitaire abstract game, it has a depth that in my opinion is comparable to many good 2-person games.  I hope that some of you will get a chance to try it.
