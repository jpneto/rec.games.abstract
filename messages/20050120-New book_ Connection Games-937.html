Hi,

The following book has just been released:

  Connection Games: Variations on a Theme
  Cameron Browne
  AK Peters, 2005
  Paperback, 416 pages, 390 illustrations
  ISBN: 1-56881-224-8
  http://akpeters.com/product.asp?ProdCode=2248

This is an encylopedia of connection games that looks at common 
themes, tactics and strategies, and examines over 200 connection 
games in detail, many new and unpublished.

Cameron
