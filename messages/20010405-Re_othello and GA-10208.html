It sounds like you are selecting only one for the others to play against.
It would probably give you better results to schedule a round-robin style
tournament and chose your breeding stock from the top scorers plus a
few chosen at random. The random start positions are a good idea also, although
I'm wondering if it might be a good idea to create new ones only once per
generation to keep good players from being eliminated by hopeless positions.
