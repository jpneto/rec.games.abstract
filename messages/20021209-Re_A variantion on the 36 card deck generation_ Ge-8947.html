Button Men?  That's what introduced the d7 (AFAIK) - they actually

"*just* the right aspect ratio" with respect to which assumptions
about the throw technique, materials of die and surface, air
resistance etc.?

In other words, the problem isn't well-defined unless you specify a
lot of assumptions.

In other other words, I wouldn't trust a d7 made as a pentagonal prism
to be fair.  I would, personally, construct a d7 as a d14 (similar to
a d10) with 1-7 twice each.  Using a d28 or higher seems OTT, and
these are the only other possibilities of a provably fair (as opposed
to probably fair) d7.
