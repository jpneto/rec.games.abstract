If I could chose from all games, I wouldn't choose any of my own
completed designs to be my desert island game. But given that
restriction and assuming there are others to play with, I'd choose
Short Changed. It's a deduction game with a strong memory element that
(in my opinion) scales smoothly from 2 to 6. It's certainly not a game
for everyone, especially those who dislike memory games and mental
math.

If I was stranded alone, however, I'd choose Piece Packing Pirates.
Neither of these games are abstract strategy games, but here are links
in case you're curious:

Short Changed: http://www.ludism.org/attachments/ppwiki/ShortChanged.pdf
Piece Packing Pirates: http://www.piecepack.org/rules/PiecePackingPirates.pdf
