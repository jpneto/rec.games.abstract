REQUEST FOR DISCUSSION: about the creation of:

    newsgroup called "rec.games.othello" (unmoderated)


PURPOSE:

This newsgroup is to be available for people who are interesting
in game OTHELLO -- players, theoreticians, programmers, organizing
tournaments, etc. 


RATIONALE:

After opening of <ios> (Internet OTHELLO Server), anonymous ftp site
with OTHELLO stuff, there seems to be need for having a news group.

To give community apropriate group for discussing things about
OTHELLO, because people were forced to post to inapropriate
rec.games.* groups.


TIMING:

    If the proponents of this newsgroup come to consensus at the end of 21
    days from the date of this article's posting to news.announce.newgroups,
    I will post a call for votes at that time.  I will shoot for a voting
    period of 21/30 days, to be handled by the Usenet Volunteer Votetakers.

All parts of this proposal are open to debate.  Let the discussion begin!
