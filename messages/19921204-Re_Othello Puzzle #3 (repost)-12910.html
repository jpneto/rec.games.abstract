Black (x) to play and win decisively ...


I would play g7. Even though white can play the corner, he is severely
limited afterwards. Black can play both g8 and h7 without hindrance,
forcing white to play b7 in the meantime. If white plays g8 or h7 the
result is much the same, except he doesn't get the corner. If white
plays b7, a8 will get the corner and quickly limits whites
possibilities.
