BTW there must be a mistake in your description of Hexmeister:

You write:

"A player (...) may not enter either Base."


and later you write:

"Winning is by Alignment or Encirclement. Alignment occurs when one player
occupies any 5 gates in a straight line (3 alternatives possible on Grid).
Encirclement occurs when one player occupies either the 6 Gates of the
Outer Ring or the Middle Ring."


However, there is only one straight line possible if a player cannot enter
the opponent's base. And you cannot occupy the 6 gates of the outer ring,
unless you enter the opponent's base.
