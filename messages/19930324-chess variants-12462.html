CLUBS:
 1) Knights of the Square Table.
      A postal games club devoted to chess, chess variants, and many other
    board games such as Xiang-Qi, Shogi (including Shogi variants such as
    Tori Shogi and Chu Shogi), Go (including Go on smaller boards), Aba-
    lone, Cathedral, and many many others.
      Also featured are word games, chess problems, and Shogi problems.
      The club members will play almost any board game that can be played
    by mail. Most notable is a game of Kriegspiel which has been in pro-
    gress for more than a decade.
~     The club publishes a bi-monthly magazine called Nost-Algia.
      Dues are: $17 a year (US), $20 a year (Canada), and $24 a year else-
    where.
      Write to:            THE NOST
                           111 Amber St.
			   Buffalo, NY 14220-1861

 2) Associazione Italiana Scacchi Eterodossi
      A postal games club based in Italy which features primarily chess
    variants and other games such as Xiang-Qi, Shogi, and Go. Sponsors an 
    international event called the Heterochess Olympics. Similar to NOST.
      The club publishes a quarterly magazine called ETEROSCACCO, in
    Italian. Dues are 25,000L per year.
      Write to:            Alessandro Castelli
 			   AISE
			   62010 Villa
			   Potenza (Macerata)
			   ITALY

  1) World Game Review -- Issue #10

     Chess" and "Les Jex d'Echecs Non Orthodoxes", among many others.  De-
     scribes over 600 variants within 100 pages. Well worth the price of $10.
       Write to:	   Michael Keller

			   3367-I North Chatham Road
