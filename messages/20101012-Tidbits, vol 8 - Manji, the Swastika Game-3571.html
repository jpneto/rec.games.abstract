Ralf Gering has selflessly nominated three of his own games in the bgg
topic, "[Which game] is the abstract of the decade?"

"Best Mancala Games: Afrika, Da Vinci's Mancala, Kauri, Manji"

Afrika is an outstanding example of a grossly non-robust "game".

Kauri is an interesting concept but it's not even finite-decisive, as
is my own Diffusion (http://www.marksteeregames.com/
Diffusion_rules.pdf), PUNTING Kauri out of the game-of-the-decade
arena.

Manji, as Ralf himself describes it, is "played in 25 pits which form
a swastika".  If you know anything about Ralf, you can see why this is
troubling.
