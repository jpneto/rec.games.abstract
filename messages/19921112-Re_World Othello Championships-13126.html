No, but they can look very deeply into the endgame.  Back in the
PDP-11 Version 6 Unix days, I wrote a Reversi program that looked
ahead through the entire (shrinking) endgame tree when there were (I

sophisticated programming than I was doing, could probably improve
this considerably.  I would hate to play against a computer that knew
the entire game tree with twenty moves left...
