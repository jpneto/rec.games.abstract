The first time you have the same distribution as with dice. As soon as
you remove cards, the probability distribution changes in favor of the
numbers that have not been drawn yet. This is an effect of the limited
size of the deck. If your deck held 360 cards instead, the diffrence
would be smaller. This is one of the reasons why casinos use decks of
this size (6 complete 52-card decks make one 312-card deck), since
this will increase their chances against card counters (or mildly
intelligent players).

It will give you less of an advantage to know that 3 aces out of 24
are out than knowing if 3 out of 4 are out. In the latter case making
a blackjack is almost impossible. They also guard their statistics by
reshuffling the complete deck after using only part of it.

In a way, they are changing blackjack from a card game to a dice game.
This is profitable again, because in a dice game the players have less
information to work with to base their strategy on.
