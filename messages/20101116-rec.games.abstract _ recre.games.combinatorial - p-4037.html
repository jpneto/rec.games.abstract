Do I object to mediocrity as a phenomenon? No. I don't object to rain
either, or reality tv. Mediocrity emerges in the wake of excellence,
in whatever field of human endeavour.

Now before you post an "there he goes again" message, let me tell you
that I suck at 99.9% of what humans regularly do, and am at best
mediocre at 99% of the rest, such as riding a bike or making a pizza.

What I object to is Mediocrity in authority, Mediocrity becoming the
norm. That's like keeping the middle to serve stratistics. A blanket
under which we all rub each other the right way.

The three most important rules in game inventing are not "politely,
politely, politely".
