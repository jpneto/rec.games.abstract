	I hosted an exchange student from Finaland once who
taught me the most fascinating game.  The nearest traslation of
it is "Timber Chess", and the rules are very simple, but he
says there are whole books written on the strategy in Finland.


	All you need is a sheet of graph paper and two
pencils.  You be Xs, get your buddy to be Os.  Just play tick
tac toe, except you can go anywhere you want, and the first
person to get five in a row wins.  It is a fantastic game.


	Mick Stone
