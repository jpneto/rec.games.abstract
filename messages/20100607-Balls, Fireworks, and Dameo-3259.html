Ralf Gering: "Emergo lacks the excitement of piece promotion, which is
essential to draughts variants. To me Emergo is a 'castrated' version
of column checkers."

Christian Freeling: "And why should having your men promoted during
the game give more exitement than having them all 'promoted' in the

being 'castrated', Emergo has a lot more balls to begin with."

Gotta go with Christian on this one.  A game of kings seems ballsier,
though I must confess I've never considered the precise testicularity
of a game.  This will be Ralf Gering's legacy.  The ball sack index.

Christian Freeling: "I don't know precisely in which year which games
