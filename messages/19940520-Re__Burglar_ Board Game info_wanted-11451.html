This sounds a lot like the Parker Brothers game Stop Thief which was
a fairly heavily-promoted game in the US.

You have the overview of a city, and every turn the electronic gizzy
makes the sounds of the thief running on different surfaces and 
opening doors. You then race around and track the invisible thief.

kinda neat game. I think that Scotland Yard is a somewhat better
game on the same theme, though. (ditto for Fury of Dracula)
