Cleaning out my closet, I found a copy of Pyramid Power. As
I recall, I got this in the mail one day with no explanation, and
with no rules. It consists of a triangular pyramid with sawtooth
patterns on the surface along each bottom edge, and red and
white tiles that can fit into the sawteeth and make patterns
built up on each of the three surfaces. Anyone have the rules?
Most of the writing on the box is in Japanese except for the
game name and 'Under license by Murray J. Gould, U.S.A.'
A Google search found a copy or two for sale in 1997 on
rec.games.marketplace but nothing about the game itself.
Thought I'd ask about it here before tossing it.
