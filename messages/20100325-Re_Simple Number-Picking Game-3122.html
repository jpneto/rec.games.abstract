A few years ago, Carl Ginnow posed a solitaire game in r.p that is
very vaguely similar to Leroy's game.
Since computer solution turned out to be very
challenging, I'll paraphrase the problem here:


1. Start with a list of the whole numbers from 1 to N, say N = 101.
2. Select a number from the list that has at least two factors
  in the list (one of the factors may be the number itself).
3. Remove all factors of the selected number from the list.
4. Repeat steps 2, 3 until all of the numbers in the list
  have only one factor remaining in the list.
.
Your score in this game is the sum of the numbers selected in step 2.
Which numbers should you select, and in which order, to maximize this sum?
