This is another solitaire game.  I'm posting it here rather than at rec.puzzles because I received zero responses there to my BoxOff post.

Clobpeg solitaire is pretty much a combination of Clobber solitaire and peg solitaire.  You fill a grid randomly with black and white stones.  The goal is to reduce the number of black stones to just one.  The number of white stones remaining doesn't matter.

A black stone can capture by replacement an adjacent white stone (as per Clobber solitaire) or it may capture by jumping an adjacent black stone (as per peg solitaire).  A white stone can't do anything.

Here's a few moves to show how it works:

b b w w
w b w b
b b b w
w b w w

A black stones captures a white stone:

b b w w
w b w b
b b _ b
w b w w

A black stone jumps a black stone:

b b w w
w b w b
_ _ b b
w b w w

A black stone captures a white stone:

b b w w
w _ b b
_ _ b b
w b w w

A black stone jumps a black stone:

b b w w
w b _ _
_ _ b b
w b w w

A black stone jumps a black stone:

b _ w w
w _ _ _
_ b b b
w b w w

And so it continues.  The basic idea is to use the white stones to get your black stones into favorable positions.
