Of course not. Discussion on the theory of games is certainly relevent to game designers. In fact, I explicitly pointed that out in the last(or second-last) sentence of my previous post. As a result, I'd expect many game designers to post in the group.

But. please understand: I want to discuss *theory* mainly. I am not particularly interested in designing games, making money, what makes a game a best-seller, etc etc. Games researchers are interested in the *research*: development and derivation of theoretical properties (mostly mathematical and computational... but social/psychological as well). The focus of the group/community would be therefore on discussions related to this, and NOT primarily about game design (like the links you gave me).

Let's put it this way: I'd like a group called alt.games.theory or alt.game.research.
