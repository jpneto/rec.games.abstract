With respect to loosing, it's one of the worst alternatives of playing, since
the quotas aren't fixed. With e.g. Roulette, you can have enough luck to make
the casino bankrupt, since they win on a statistical basis. With lotto, even
if every player has all 6 numbers correct, the lotto company still makes money
because they simply return only a fixed percentage of the money to the winners.
