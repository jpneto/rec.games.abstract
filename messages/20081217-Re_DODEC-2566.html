DODEC
=====
Played on the vertices of a dodecahedron, (or cells of an icosa),
where one face's interior has been deleted.

The opening player fills in one vertex with his colour,
then players successively fill two vertices per turn,
until the board is full.

When play has ended, each of the 19 faces is coloured
by the majority colour of its surrounding vertices.

Whoever has 10 or more faces is the winner.
