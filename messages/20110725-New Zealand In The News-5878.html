New Zealand Bans Weird Baby Names

The country's Registrar of Births, Deaths and Marriages has been cracking
down on parents who get too creative when naming their kids, Australia's
Herald Sun reports.

The list of weird names for kids that are banned by New Zealand's names
registrar has grown to include Lucifer, Duke, Bill Taylor, Messiah and 89.
