Volo is a connection game inspired by the movement of flocks of birds.
It plays somewhat like Hex, but with limited rearrangement of the
pieces allowed; but with elements of Lines of Action mixed in. The
rules are pretty simple - you either add a new "bird" or move a line
of birds in a way that enlarges the flock without splitting it.
