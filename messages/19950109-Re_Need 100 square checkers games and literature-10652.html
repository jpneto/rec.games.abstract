: Please can someone send me good games of 100 square checkers. I have

An excellent book to get on a variety of games is R. Wayne
Schmittberger's  _New Rules for Classic Games_.  I saw it once at Barnes
& Noble in the Bargain section for $5.  (Every gamer should own this
book, if only for ideas that can be applied for other games.)

I t includes rules for wide variety games  (From chess variants to
"kickball Golf").  There are 3 excellent chapters on chess, checkers and
backgammon.   There are many great games that involve making a board or
other easily improvised equipment.

This is a rules book, not a strategy guide.  As for strategy, there are
many checkers variants ( each country seems to have a different one), so any
strategy guide for say Spanish checkers won't necessarily apply to any
other others.  But good luck on your search.
