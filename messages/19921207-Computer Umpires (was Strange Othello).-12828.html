In article <1992Dec7.121443.9762@odin.diku.dk>, torbenm@diku.dk (Torben
AEgidius Mogensen) made a number of interesting suggestions involving
various extensions to Othello. Many of these involved the addition of
rules which complicate the game somewhat.

What opinions do people hold regarding the use of computers as umpires
in abstract games?

Would having a computer as umpire extend the acceptable complexity of
abstart games?

How do people feel about playing games whose rules they do not fully
understand?

Has anybody written a computer game which involves guessing computer
generated rules?
