All designers are given to outlandish claims now and again.  


Case in point.  Hex KB, so named for the famous four dimensional Klein
bottle.  My outlandish claim was that Hex KB was a 4D game.  One day a
guy emails me questioning the veracity of my claim.  At first I said
"Damn straight it's 4D."  An hour later I said "Er, uh..."  An hour
after that I apologized for my error, realizing that Hex KB is in fact
three dimensional.  I changed my literature to "inspired by the Klein
bottle".

It's called honesty, Christian.  Give it a try some time.
