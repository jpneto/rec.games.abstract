I wonder if by any chance someone got the rule of the Fanurna.
It is a game from Madagascar that you play on a small wooden
board 9x5 holes where you put small stones. You can move the
stones only along the lines printed in the wood. Not every
move between the different holes is allowed. Then I think
you can take your oppponent's stones only if there is one gap
between his and yours but I don't remember everything. I'll
be glad if someone could help me to get back my travel memories!
