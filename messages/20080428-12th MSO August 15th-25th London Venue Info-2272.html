The 12th MSO UK runs from Friday evening Aug 15th through to Aug 25th 2008. 
The venue is
Royal Horticultural Halls, 80 Vincent Square, (Westminster) London SW1P 2PE, 
10 minutes from Victoria railway and underground station , Pimlico and St 
James's Park tube, fifteen minutes from Westminster tube.

More details will be available on the MSO website http://msoworld.com at 
some point, though last year's schedule on that site gives an idea of the 
games likely to be played.

Hopefully this Central London Venue will attract more competitors from the 
rest of Europe & the wider world.
