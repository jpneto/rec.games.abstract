SBdDbC stands for:

    Single Board, Double Chess

You need a single chess board, two sets
of chess pieces and 32 checkers.


16 checkers of each color.


You can make only a single short castling
and/or a single long castling (always with single
pieces, even if king or the respective rook
are double -- thus castling would undouble them).


You may make short castling with one king,
and if it means undoubling your king, then
you may still make long castling with the
king left on e1 or e8 depending on the side.
(Otherwise, the standard castling rules
are adopted). The same goes for the long
first and short castling later.


Enjoy.
