Information-geek speak. A lossy channel is one where you are not
necessarily able to use the full bandwidth of the communications
medium. Specifically, in Bridge, you have the opposing team
interfering with your ability to communicate with you partner. Makes
it kind of fun, but it's definitely *not* a single person game.
