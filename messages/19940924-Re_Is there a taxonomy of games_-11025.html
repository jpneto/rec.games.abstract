The taxonomy of ideas is always difficult. Where does one place "Racing 
Kings", for instance? This is a Race game played with chess pieces, using 
many of the standard rules of chess. The trading-card games are more like 
war games than like card games, but just ask about them on 
rec.games.board some time. 

The limitations of taxonomy of ideas transcend any one specific taxonomy. 
No matter what lines you draw, similar games are going to be grouped 
apart and dissimilar games will be grouped together. A taxonomy by 
designer will group "Mousetrap" with "Acquire" and "Can't Stop", or "Web 
and Starship" with "Toon". A taxonomy by pure-strategy vs. elements of 
luck will group "Acquire" with "Snakes and Ladders" or "Chess" with 
"Tic-Tac-Toe".

satisfying as any I have seen), order WGR #1 and #3 from:
