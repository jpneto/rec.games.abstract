Of the three variable orientation cell division connection games presented so far, Tripen is by far my (admittedly biased) favorite.  It uses a standard, honeycomb tessellation, and the only impurity is an asymmetric token.  Something has to give in the pursuit of VOCD (vehement obsessive compulsive disorder).
