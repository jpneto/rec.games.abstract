Soar is my entry in the Stacking Games Contest:
http://mindsports.nl/index.php/the-pit/soar-574

It was designed to fit a specific family: elimination games that start
out on an empty board and have no hard wired distinction between an
entering- and a moving stage. Games in that category include Nine
Men's Morris, Morabaraba, Yoté, Oust and Emergo. Here's how the design
process took place:
http://mindsports.nl/index.php/how-i-invented-games-and-why-not/late-arrivals-a-final-whispers#soar

Soar has 'soft finitude': and endgame between two single men is a win
for the player who has 'the move' which in Soar's case means 'can move
into diagonal opposition'.

Soar can be played at MindSports: registration is free and after
registration the game can be selected in the 'preferences'. Players
then can challenge others or be challenged by them.
http://mindsports.nl/index.php/players-section
