History, rules and list of pc games to the board game Pachisi & Ludo.

http://home.no.net/vkp3/index.html

The site lists 12 online, 36 freeware / abandonware and 25 commercial games.

Pachisi = 'Twenty-five'. Descendants include Ludo (England) and Parcheesi (USA). Ludo is also known as Mensch Ärgere dich nicht (Germany), Mens-erger-je-niet (the Netherlands), Parchís or Parkase (Spain), Le Jeu de Dada or Petits Chevaux (France), Non t'arrabbiare (Italy), Fia med knuff (Sweden), Parqués (Colombia).
