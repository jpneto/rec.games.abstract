My response to Bill:

Thanks Bill.  The move restrictions in hex hex Flume are
an anti-meatball strategy.  Players growing a dense meatball
of stones, or a lump on the perimeter isn't very interesting.  I
wanted to put move restrictions in square Flume as well, but
they did more harm than good.  On the bright side, square
Flume ends up being a very simple game.
