Love to play games but tired of Monopoly? Come and try the world's
best board and card games! Batty's Best Game Fest will be a chance for
fans of European-style games such as Settlers of Catan and Blokus to
gather for fun and fellowship. No RPGs, CCGs or wargames will be
played. Families are welcome, but children under 16 must be
accompanied by an adult. Members of the Atlanta Board Game Group will
be on hand to help with rules and get everyone playing and having fun.
Memberships are $7 for one-day and $11 for both days.
