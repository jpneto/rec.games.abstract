For those of you who are interested, I thought I'd post a listing of upcoming
Othello tournaments in the USA.  These tournaments are run by the United
States Othello Association (USOA).  Please let me know if you're interested
in future postings of Othello tournaments -- if nobody is, I won't do it
again.

In the following tournaments, play begins at 10AM, with registration at
9:30AM.  I strongly encourage those interested and able to play to participate.
They should be lots of fun!


The 1993 U.S. Open Othello tournament will be held in New York City on
Saturday, April 17, 1993.  The tournament site will be The Williams Club,
24 E. 39th Street (between Madison and Park), NY NY (212) 697-5300.
$25 entrance fee -- $100 prize for first place, $50 class prizes.  For
further information, contact David Parsons, 7 Peter Cooper Rd #10G, NY NY
10010, tel # (212) 475-8911.

The 1993 Virginia Open will be held Saturday, May 22nd in the Community
Room at the Fashion Square Mall, Charlottesville, VA 22901.  Rookies play
free.  For more information, call John Stoner at 804-978-4724.

The 1993 Harrisburg (PA) Open will be held Saturday, June 12th at the
B'nai B'rith apartments, 130 South 3rd St.  For maps or more information
contact Scott Portzline, 717-232-8863.
