I don't believe that an optimal strategy for Mastermind is known.
 From what I recall, good strategies are ones where the guess tends to
split the remaining possibilities in half.
That suggests a best case (I realize you're not after best case)
that's log base 2 w/r/t the number of pegs.  Search for the paper "Yet
Another Mastermind Strategy" for some ideas.
