Soon after releasing Palisade I began to have my doubts about the game
so I withdrew it from my portfolio.  A number of players vehemently
objected to the recall.  Since then the potential for strategy in
Palisade has been convincingly demonstrated to me and I feel
comfortable endorsing Palisade once again.  Palisade will remain in my
portfolio from this time forward.
