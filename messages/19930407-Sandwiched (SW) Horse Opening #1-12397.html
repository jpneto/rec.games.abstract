Hi, this is the first part of the Sandwiched (SW) Horse opening series. This
opening was first used by Chinese players way back in the 1950's. However,
it did not catch on with the players as most games were won by Red's central
Cannon opening. It was only till late 1960's that Hu Rong Hua (again!)
started experimenting with this opening. It was a considerable success as he
won many games playing Black and it finally caught the attention of the
other Xiangqi players.

In the 1st first part to this series, I shall describe the 'early' openings
which Hu used in the 60's.

SW Horse #1
