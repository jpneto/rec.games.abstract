 From Option 1, we see that Black's Rook wasted many moves to exchange with
Red's inactive Rook. Red's Horses are active now and Red's opening is
successful.

Option 2:
6.  H3+4!  R43
7.  H7-5!! C5+4
8.  H4+6   R34
9.  H6-5   R45
10. H5+3   R5-1
11. R2+6

Again, in Option 2, Red sacrifices two Soliders in exchange for more active
Horses. Red's opening is successful.

 From the above 2 options, we realise that Black's Rook has moved too often
in the opening. Hence there is a need for Black to develop his pieces first
before attacking Red:
