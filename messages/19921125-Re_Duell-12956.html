In a previous article, Daniel Lemon asked about Duell:

Just what is the proper orientation for the dice in their starting
position?


I don't have the game, so this might not be right.

     5 2 1 6 K 6 1 2 5

With 4 showing on the sides facing you.

On your first move, you should be able to threaten the king with either of the
outside pieces (where the fives are).  If you can, then I'm confident that
they are in the correct position.  I am not sure about the position of the two
and one, they might be reversed.  And rather than fours facing you, it could be
threes.  The king is definitely correct.  I'm also fairly sure about the sixes.
