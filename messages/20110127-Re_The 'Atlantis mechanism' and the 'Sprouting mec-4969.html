The chess variant Renaissance by Matthew Montchalin -- 1975 -- also
features a piece called the resurrector that "gives birth" to a piece
from the reserve as it moves.  Pieces go into the reserve in the
manner of shogi, a captured opposing piece changing sides.  The
resurrector is not the royal piece, though.
