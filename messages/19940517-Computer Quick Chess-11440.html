Does anyone know if there is a computer version of Quick Chess exists?
Barney Pell's METAGAME could certainly play it, but as I currently don't
have access to Prolog I can't use it (don't know how strong a player it
would be, either).

For those who don't know, Quick Chess is played on a 5x6 board.
No castling, no initial 2-space pawn moves allowed.  The initial
setup looks like this:

         6 r b k q n
         5 p p p p p
         4 - - - - -
         3 - - - - -
         2 P P P P P
         1 R B K Q N
           a b c d e

Any decent program should be quite strong at this.
