If you are compiling Hex literature you might want to also include 'Y', which
is a related game played on a triangular board, where you have to connect all
three sides.  (Not quite triangular lattice, three of the points connect to
only five, not six points)  For info, check out the June 94 issue of Games
magazine, which has a review. (and a photo!)
