During the month of December, Boardspace.net will host
the first annual year-end Lines of Action tournament.

Tournament games will be played in realtime (not by email)
at times agreed between the players.  The overall format
will be small group, probably 5 player, qualifying rounds;
followed by an elimination tournament among the winners of
the qualifiers.

To sign up, visit this page:

http://boardspace.net/cgi-bin/tournament-signup.cgi
