It is pretty unlikely that you would get a positive vote for a (big 8) group
like this out of the blue. What you want to do is try to get some dicscussion
going in related groups (possibly rec.games.board or rec.games.abstract)
and if enough traffic seems to warrant a separate group then start trying
to get one created. You could create and alt group, but they don't get
as good of propagation as big 8 groups.

You probably would find some interest in these topics in rec.games.board.
Many of the specific topics you have mentioned have been discussed there
in the past.

I am trying to move this discussion there (followups set) as you are more
likely to see interest there than here.
