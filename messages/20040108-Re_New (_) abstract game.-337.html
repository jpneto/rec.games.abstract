heads die when they can't move (get surrounded).

game ends when the worm no head can move.   last player to make a valid move wins.

if you extend it to N worms (1 per player, not a common worm/worms), then it becomes more of a lightcycle duel.  early parts of the game trying to carve out as much territory as you can while while trying to keep your surrounded space open enough to fill in slowly.
