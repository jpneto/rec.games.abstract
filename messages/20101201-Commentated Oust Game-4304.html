Rod Jackson strikes again with a commentated Oust game - a program that lets
you step through the moves of an example Oust game.  Notes relevant to some
of the moves appear under the board.

The Oust theory presented therein might be a little advanced.  Opening Oust
play is counterintuitive and the novice may be wondering why players refrain
from making obvious kills.  Better to play Oust a few times and then view
the step-through.  Rod's the best Oust player in the world, so like I said,
it's a little advanced.
