After thinking about this for a few minutes, I agree that it would be good to
choose games that are widely varied and don't take terribly long to play,
thereby lending themselves to tournament play.

Among traditional games, I nominate:
  1. Crokinole
  2. Sprouts
  3. Chinese Checkers
  4. Qelat Mancala
  5. Reversi

Among modern games, I nominate:
  1. Chase
  2. Tanbo
  3. Waving Hands
  4. Trax
  5. Button Men*

* While this isn't an abstract game in the strictest sense (it involves dice
rolls to see who goes first and after captures), it has great potential for
tactical thought.
