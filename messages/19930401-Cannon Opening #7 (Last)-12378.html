Though Red pieces are more active, nevertheless, it is not easy to win the
game as Black's defence is solid. It will depend on the end-game skills of
both sides to decide the outcome of the game.

The main advantage of SW Horse is it can consolidate its defences very early
by moving the Assistants and Elephants. Moreover, Red cannot move H8+7 now
since Black can pin by C6+5. Subsequently, Red's attack is more subdued.

However, Black's setback is its Rooks cannot move into play very quickly.
Also, since there is essentially 1 Horse guarding the central Black Soldier,
Black tends to lose the central soldier in the middle game. To offset this,
Black must activate all its pieces faster and be adept in launching counter
attacks.
