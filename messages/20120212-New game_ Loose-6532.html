In the last days, spurred by Mark's success in Red, I've revisited my
old project of designing the ideal finite Go variant, and Loose has
been the result of it.

I think it's significant improvement on both Reversi Go and my own
Goncrete, and may well be close to my ideal. The only added
complication with respect to Reversi Go is the distinction between
"true" and "false" liberties, yet the game is very different. Players
no longer have to take any specific eye creation steps upon captures,
as eyes are a natural result of the capturing mechanism.

Only Black and White stones are used. They are flipped when they run
out of true liberties, but never removed from the board.
