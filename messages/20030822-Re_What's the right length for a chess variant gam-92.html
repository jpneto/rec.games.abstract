A chess variant that lasts 30-45 moves is very appropriate.  Tastes vary, and
there are popular variants that average less than 10 moves (Progressive) and
over a hundred (large Shogi variants, for example).  Your game should appeal to
plenty of players, at least as far as game length goes.
