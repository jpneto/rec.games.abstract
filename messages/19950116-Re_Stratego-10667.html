Stratego is an elegant and fun game.  But I've never been quite
sure how to interpret and/or generalize the rule prohibiting
repetitions, i.e. the one that reads:
"No piece may be shifted more than 5 times back and forth in the same
2 squares."




The old rules were more explicit, and called for no more than 3. Basically, 
however, what don't you get? If i move left, right, left, right, left, right,
what have i done? violated the rule. similarly, r,l,r,l,r,l or forward, back,
forward, back, forward, back, or b,f,b,f,b,f. It is only to keep people from
running back and forth with a threatened piece and trying for a draw that way,
and it is a good rule. As you may intuit, the person who starts moving back and
forth has to break the pattern first.
