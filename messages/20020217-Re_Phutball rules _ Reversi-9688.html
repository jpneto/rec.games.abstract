I think that's correct. However, not all games are implemented correctly
on your PBeM server. Reversi is  not given with the correct rules:

"If a player runs out of discs, but still has an opportunity to outflank
an opposing disc on his or her turn, the opponent must give the player a
disc to use."

According to K. Handscomb in Abstract Games 9 and my German gamebooks from
Ravensburg (all written well before the advent of Othello), no pieces are
exchanged. Ravensburg actually owned the copyright for the game.

According to Ravensburg the first player plays two pieces in his first
move (and so decides which opening pattern is chosen) and then the second
player completes the pattern by playing the other two pieces of the
opening position. The opening pattern is created in two moves, not in
four.
