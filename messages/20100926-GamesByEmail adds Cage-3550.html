GamesByEmail adds MSG game, Cage.

http://www.gamesbyemail.com/

http://www.marksteeregames.com/Cage_rules.html

GamesByEmail is a simple but awesome turn based site that's fully
cellphone compliant.  You just select a game, put your email and
someone else's email and you're good to go.  The site automatically
creates an account for you with that email.

Proprietor Scott Nesin is a master of the game interface.  In his Cage
implementation, for example, you perform multiple jumps in one
sweeping motion of your checker over the enemy checkers.  Smooth as
silk.  Scott's attention to detail is astonishing.
