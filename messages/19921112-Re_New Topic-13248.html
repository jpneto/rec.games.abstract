Yes!

Here in Trondheim we sometimes play a RPG called 'IMPRO' (short for
improvisation).  You start out with a die (you need it once).  The player
who throws the lowes number is the GM.  Then you start playing.  The players,
the GM and others normaly don't agree on where they are.  This including
time, space, world etc.  Players normaly have whatever they want, but the 
GM always have something better (if he wants).
The game works all rigth, but demands people who are not shy :-)
