Half of those 5 pages are just examples, here:

4x regular hexgrid starts out full with one unit type,
2 steps per turn (1 on very first for balance),
steps can be either direct removals or movements,
you can directly remove a single completely-surrounded unit, or
you can move any single unit in a straight line so long as it jumps at least one other unit and has at least one adjacent unit at the destination,
if there is more than one group after a movement only the largest remains (splitter chooses in case of equals),
at least one unit must be removed every turn either directly or by splitting or you lose,
when only two units are left in play whichever player has removed more units is the winner
