Usually annotations from advanced players are available only to master level
SR Chess players.  But with the loosening of the restrictions governing the
publication of annotated Stanley Random Chess games, and in the interests of
promoting the game, a rare annotated exhibition game between two current
grandmasters is now available to the public.

This exciting SR Chess exhibition game between Austin and GM Topov is
currently in progress, and can be followed live at the following location:
http://www.schemingmind.com/game.aspx?game_id=5786

Future exhibitions games will be made available here:
http://www.geocities.com/verdrahciretop/src8.html
