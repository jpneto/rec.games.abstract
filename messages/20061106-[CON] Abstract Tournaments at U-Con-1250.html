The U-Con gaming convention will be held at the University of Michigan Union in Ann Arbor, MI the weekend of 17-19 November 2006:

    www.ucon-gaming.org

As of this writing, there are four scheduled tournament events for abstract strategy games:

Friday, 17 November, 7:00 pm to 10:00 pm -- Michigan Azacru Open
Saturday, 18 November, 10:00 am to 2:00 pm -- Chess Variants
Saturday, 18 November, 2:00 pm to 6:00 pm -- Various Abstracts
Sunday, 19 November, 12:00 m to 4:00 pm -- Michigan Pacru Open

A weekend pass for the entire convention is only US$20. Single day passes are US$15 for Saturday or Sunday, and US$10 for Friday. In addition to that, the entry fees for each of the above tournaments is only US$3.

I hope to see you there!
