simple such that creating the gameplay and rules are at a kindergarden
stage, however, it provides a great challenge when it comes to creating
the player and AI interaction within the game (player vs. computer).

This sort of challenge will assist when it comes to creating an AI in a
further game, which I am sure it will be needed.

which will then be followed by the intended game.
