Announcing the First Email TRAX Tournament!!!

That's right, you now have a chance to show off your TRAX skill against
other players from around the globe.  All levels from "just learning"
to "world champion" are welcome.  To register, just send email by July
4th, 1993 to munch@soda.berkeley.edu with the exact subject:

Subject: trax: <username>: <password>

For "username" use a name you'd like to be called, up to 10 characters
in all lower case.

For "password" use an 8 character password with no spaces or colons.

Example:

Subject: trax: mel: secret

(This would register "mel" for play with the password "secret")

Registration open as soon as soda comes back up from some unscheduled
maintainance (probably before you get this news).  You should receive
confirmation of your registration by mail soon after you send to munch.

Pairings will be brazillian style with group size determined by number
participating.
