British Othello Federation: Regional tournaments 1994

These tournaments are open to all human players. Of those who have not 
previously qualified for this year's British championships, the top thee 
finishers at each event will qualify.

Worksop: April 23rd.
Nottingham: May 7th.
Manchester: May 14th.
Cambridge: June 11th.
Portsmouth: June 18th.
Doncaster: July 23rd.

North vs South match.

There will be a team match, North vs South, in Nottingham on July 9th 1994.
Places are still available in both teams.

European Grand Prix tournaments.

Copenhagen: April 23-24.
Rome May 21-22.
Brussels: 30-31 July.
Paris: August 27-28.
