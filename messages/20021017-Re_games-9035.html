There is no one best site. It all depends what kind of game you want to
play.

www.playsite.com
www.ludoteka.com
www.brettspielwelt.de

will take you in the right direction if you like to play 2-player games
other than chess, checkers, reversi... online in real-time.

There are also several other servers, that make it possible to play a single
game online, such as
www.zone.com/trax
www.octi.net
www.abalonegames.com
www.tantrix.com
www.mambagame.com
www.pente.org

I am sure there are a few more that i forgot...

If your gaming needs are more obscure than these sites, you can also try
www.zillionsofgames.com. At this site you will find a program with modules
for mostly any game you can think of.

If you like a major game, such as chess, checkers, Go, you may want a
specialized server instead, such as kgs.kiseido.com for Go.

If you like to play over email, a good starting point is
www.gamerz.net/pbmserv and www.itsyourturn.com but there are several more
email servers too.
