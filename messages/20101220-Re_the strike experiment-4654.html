I just played Charisma with Daniel and it seemed like an awesome game to me.
 The highly unique capturing mechanism gets used a lot.  It's not like
Cannon, where you get to "fire the cannon" two or three times if you're
lucky before it turns into Breakthrough.  In Charisma, the defining
mechanism really is central.  At first glance it seems a little mathematical
but it becomes totally natural like three moves into your first game.
