I finally played a game of this. First impression: good.  I like the
fact that, when capturing by filling a true liberty, you don't make an
eye for yourself.  Making eyes and capturing (by true liberties) are
separate efforts, which reduces the advantage gained by a traditional
Go capture. I like this because it seems to reduce the positive
feedback inherent in capture, which in turn adds subtlety

Grain of salt though: I'm a poor Go player and Go-understander and my
impressions could be way off.
