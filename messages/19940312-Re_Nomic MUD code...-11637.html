Nomic World was a (now defunct) MUD for playing Nomic.  There are
currently no Nomic MUDs left;  however, there are currently two
mailing list for playing Nomic that I know of.

I am playing in nomic@yoyo.cc.monash.edu.au, and am Ambassador
for that game;  that is why you are hearing from me.  :)

There is another game called Tabula running at nomic@reed.edu.

Also, there is a Nomic variant called Fantasy Rules running on
a list, frc@nvg.unit.no.

One reaches the former at listserv@yoyo.cc.monash.edu.au and sending

SUB nomic your-name

The Reed list is reached I believe at nomic-request@reed.edu; the Fantasy
Rules Committee is reached at frc-request@nvg.unit.no.

Both the game at yoyo and the FRC are descendants of the original game
at Nomic World.
