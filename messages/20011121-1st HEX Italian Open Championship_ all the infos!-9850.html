During Italian Mind Sport Olympiad ( http://www.msoworld.com/italy ) there
will be the:

1st HEX ITALIAN OPEN CHAMPIONSHIP

Time: Saturday, December 8th, 12.00 a.m. (please sign for the tournament
before 11.30 a.m.)
Place: room G, Stelline Palace, C.so Magenta #61, Milano, Italia
Cost: Nothing. Nada. Nulla. Gratis. Free.

Rules:
1) Tournament will be Round Robin or Swiss, according to the number of
players.
2) Time limit: 15 minutes for player.
3) Boards will be 11*11.
4) "Swap" rule (also known as "Pie" rule) will be used to avoid 1st move
advantage.
5) The tournament is open to everyone. But non-italian players can only win
the tournament, and not "Hex Italian Champion" title... 8-)
6) Hex-playing software is ammitted. But software will be excluded from
prizes and titles. Software authors must provide a notebook and a person to
run the program.

For any question or for sign in the tournament, please contact Mr. Maurizio
De Leo madeleo@tiscalinet.it
To obtain further infos about Italian Mind Sport Olympiad
http://www.msoworld.com/italy
