I'm already finding the list of games in the contest that need to be
test played formidable.  Tossing a shot-from-the-hip game on the pile
isn't working for me. 

Some games, like Atoll, don't need play testing. Creature Feature
does. If there's some flaw that can be uncovered with a few quick run-
throughs on a small board, that's legwork that should be done by you,
not redundantly by everyone in the contest. 

I'm calling for a little designer effort in this case and for all
entries. I want to see a decent rule sheet with some graphics and some
examples. And a brief outline of what to expect from the gameplay. 

If you expect a bunch of people to invest their time evaluating your
game, you have an obligation to invest some time in the game yourself
up front.
