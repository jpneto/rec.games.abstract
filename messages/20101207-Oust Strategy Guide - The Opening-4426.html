Oust Strategy Guide - The Opening

Rod Jackson has done an outstanding job putting this Oust strategy guide
together.
  http://shogiwar.webs.com/Hex_Oust/index.html

Lots of diagrams and links to the terminology page, with more diagrams.

Just looking at the The Opening
  http://shogiwar.webs.com/Hex_Oust/Opening.html

In particular the first small diagram with two black stones and a white
stone.  Beginners, watch out for that.  It puts White into the unenviable
position of either having to kill one black stone with the other still out
of reach, or allowing Black to form a 3 group if White does nothing.

I guess the moral here is, Don't kill anything unless you can get a 3 group
in doing so.  You don't want to kill and end up with only a 2 group.  It
isn't worth it.
