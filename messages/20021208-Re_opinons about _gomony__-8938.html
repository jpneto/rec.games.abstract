On 06 Dec 2002 01:40:21 GMT, mkgfabo@aol.com (Mkgfabo) wrote:


Has anyone played "Gomony" or read any reviews?  I'm considering buying a used
copy, if the game is any good.



Don't waste your money.   I bought a copy from the "inventor",
Harvey Klein, on eBay in 1998 for six dollars.    The description is
not exactly false, but you'd assume from it that it's a new and
original game.   I decided six dollars was too little to be worth
pursuing a complaint with eBay.  It's cheaply made, with a
standard checkerboard and checkers of four colors glued together.

It's exactly the same as Lasca, except that Gomony is played on an
8x8 board and Lasca is 7x7.   You can find rules for Lasca easily on
the Internet (e.g. <http://www.lasca.co.uk/>) or in books (e.g.
Wayne Schmittberger's _New_Rules_For_Classic_Games_ or
David Pritchard's _Brain_Games_).   It is supposed to be a pretty
good game, although Schmittberger says Emergo is better.
