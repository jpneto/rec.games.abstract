Automatism --

A real reason some of us in our collective crania wanted to procreate this group
into existence.

     "Solitary and collective automatic techniques, and the exploitation of
_chance_ are central to many surrealist games. The original surrealists soon
came to realize the limitations of *pure automatism.* Automatic techniques may
be used as a *beginning* of creative activity, to stimulate and encourage
spontaneity of utterance or image making."

We would like to start some mass induction of group-created, automatic art.
Simple games such as "The Exquisite Corpse," and others used by surrealists can
be readily adapted to distribution and propagation on the net. With some working
we hope to do the same with graphic art leaving this or perhaps another created
group, if the interest exists, for the posting of surrealistic imagery or of
works created automatically by group effort or by individuals. Once again,
though overlap certainly exists with alt.nonsequitur, alt.stupidity, etc. PLEASE
do not use this group as a repository for things that you would post to these
other groups.

Automatism aside we certainly hope that this group will serve as an ideal forum
for discussing surrealism, it's role in shaping history, techniques used,
psychology or lack thereof and not just end up as a repository of the
surrealistically inspired.

If anyone has any ideas how to organize groups for playing "net games of the
surreal," or for posting imagery please post them here. Such things should be
for mass discussion before we attempt them.

Those who would be interested in participating in any "games" we set or have
suggestions for games to try can drop me a note at lynn@pharmdec.wustl.edu.
