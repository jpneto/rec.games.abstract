I am keen to hear from anyone who plays Trax, or knows of anyone who plays
Trax or even knows of a Trax club.

Trax is a strategy game played with red tiles with white and black lines as
shown here: 
		    ______________      _____________
		   |......##......|    |.....| |.....|
	     one   |......##......|    |..../ /......|  other
	     side  |______##______|    |__/ /........|  side
	     of    |______##______|    |___/.....####|  of
	     tile  |......##......|    |......####...|  tile
		   |......##......|    |.....##......|
		   |______##______|    |_____##______|

Approximately 10,000 sets have been sold in the U.S. of A. and 30,000 in
Spain. There is also a large number of sets in Japan.

I am looking for players for international tournaments and to advance the 
game generally.

If you are looking for a excellent strategy game, then maybe this is for you.
The rules are as simple to play as Draughts (Checkers) yet the level of
strategy can be as high as that of chess.

Comparison with chess (for instance):
  Simpler rules
  Draws are not possible (using tournament rules)
  No board is required - any flat surface will do
  As the game progresses the position gets larger and more complex, whereas
    chess gets simpler as pieces are removed.
