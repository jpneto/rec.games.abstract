Hi.  I have a question probably answerable by a game programmer...

Given a random number generating function which always provides the same
sequence of numbers, is there a mathematical technique to generate truly
random numbers?


   No.

   Buy why do you need truly random numbers?  If you can come up with
specs detailing the properties you need (time before the numbers repeat,
correlation between numbers, etc.) you should always be able to come up
with an algorithm that is close enough to random to suit your needs.
