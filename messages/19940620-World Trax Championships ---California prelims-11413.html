Trax, the popular New Zealand boardless board game, is having it's World
Tournament.  If you live east of the Mississipi, then your "local" prelim
site is San Leandro, California (on the east side of the San Francisco
bay area).  Games will take place on Saturday the 16th of July.

Contact Mel Nicholson at mhn@netcom.com for details if you play Trax and
want to participate.
