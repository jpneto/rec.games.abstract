Knightmare Chess (with a "knight" not "night") is a set of cards
from Steve Jackson Games to augment your chess game.  It's
basically the English edition of Ludodelire's Tempete sur
l'Echiquier (designed by Bruno Faidutti).  See

http://www.sjgames.com/knightmare/

for full info.
