On Tue, 30 Jul 2002 08:46:31 +0100, christophe <cb@toodoo.net> wrote:


Could someone who knows better than I this game, explain me if there are 
 tactics for Kahuna ? and how to minimize the matter of random in the 
game. Many Thanks.


The single most important tactic in Kahuna is to place your bridges so
that you force the removal of your opponents bridges. Note that this
is rather different than the "obvious" strategy of taking over an
island. Obviously you need to control islands but you want to delay
doing so until such a point that controlling it removes your opponents
bridges. 

Generally speaking, good players will perform several turns where they
simply draw cards. When they do play it will often be all/most of
their cards.
