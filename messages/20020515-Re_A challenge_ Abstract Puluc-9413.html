I wonder if it is possible to remove the luck factor out of Puluc.
Ideas?


I'm tempted to say, just looking, that removing the luck factor
might force one player or the other to have an advantage.  Here's
one suggestion that might fix:

If there are currently no "stacks," a player must place and/or
move a total of four tokens and/or spaces. Otherwise, a player
moves a number of spaces/tokens equal to the tallest stack of
the opponent.

A game I've been working on, Kechi, has each player moving a
total of six places among his/her four tokens, and that seems
to be enough to keep the game interesting--of course, my game
functions in 2-D, unlike Puluc.
