Op dinsdag 8 januari 2013 03:00:36 UTC+1 schreef Mark Steere het volgende:

The pie rule isn't intended to make a game perfectly balanced.  


Maybe it's good to note why the pie rule is called the pie rule. It got its name from the procedure to divide a piece of cake between two children: let one make the cut and let the other have first choice.

Saying it is a perfect procedure does not mean that a piece of cake can be cut perfectly - it can't. 
It means that neither has reason to complain.
