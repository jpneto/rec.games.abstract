Hello,

I'm looking for the winning configurations for a game called "Sticks 'N'
Barrels".  The game is played on a 7 x 7 grid and each of the two players
has ten sticks, four solid barrels, and four hollow barrels.  On each turn
a single piece is played.  A stick and a hollow barrel can occupy the
same grid position.  To win, you must arrange the sticks and barrels
(of a single color) in one of several possible configurations.  The
configurations are all some combination of sticks and barrels appearing
five in a row either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

I'm looking for the complete list of winning configurations.  For example,
I think one one of them is:

Stick-Barrel, Barrel, Stick-Barrel, Barrel, Stick-Barrel

Can someone provide the complete list?
