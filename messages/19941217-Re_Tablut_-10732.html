A long discussion of Tablut (and the larger game Hnefaltafl) can be found 
in Wayne Schmittberger's _New Rules for Classic Games_ (John Wiley & 
Sons, 1992, 0-471-53621-0). He uses Tablut as the quintessential example 
of an entertaining but unbalanced game and illustrates several principles 
of game balancing with it, including move bidding and 1- and 3- move 
equalization.
