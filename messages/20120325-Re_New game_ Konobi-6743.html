.. . . . .
.. . . . .
x a o . x
.. b x . .
.. . x . .

'a' is a legal move for Black. It's only weakly connected to c2, but all the available placements (b2 and d2) which would be strongly connected to it would also be weakly connected to some other stones.

'b' is an illegal move for Black. It's only weakly connected to a3, and it's still possible to make strong connections to it (on a2 and a4) without making weak connections to other stones.
