Hello to all,
I wrote here some months ago to advertise my hobby program,
Nomignolov. Since more than a half year passed, and I improved my
program in a number of ways, here are some information.

My program handles any game described via the Lua scripting
language. By "handles", I mean that it shows to the user the board and
the pieces, and take the user's inputs to update the board if the move
is possible.

Nomv can play at random or with a weak AI (which depends on the
script of the game).

In the zip there are the scripts for the following games/solitaries:
Dao, Entropy (by Carreno), Conway's Soldiers, Wari, Tic tac toe,
Diffusion (by Mark Steere), 14-15 (puzzle by Sam Loyd), 3verse, Hex,
Tablut.

You can download Nomv, and read the rules of 3verse, from
nomignolov.blogspot.com

In the zip there is a documentation (a pdf of 11 pages) with
complete information about writing a script for a new game.

I greatly appreciate any comment, suggestion, bug report, etc.

Thank you again for your attention!
