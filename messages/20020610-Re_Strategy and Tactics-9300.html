There quite a bit more to othello strategy than that.  Trying to
assign values to specific types of squares is a rather poor long
term strategy.



: I don't understand what you mean by "superficial".

Just that I'm often lacking alternative goals for getting

I would say getting a corner is *usually* good.  There are situations
where grabbing a corner is the wrong thing to do because doing so
gives
away more to your opponent (e.g. grabbing a corner might allow your
opponent to "wedge in" next to the corner and take an entire side.)
