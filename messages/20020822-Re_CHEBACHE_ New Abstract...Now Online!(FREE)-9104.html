Unfortunately, it is quite impossible to descirbe the games without
diagrams. The game is essentially an involved variant of Backgammon, with
extra pieces and rules to make it more interesting.

Jumping pieces (from checkers).

A king piece (from chess) which is extra powerful (resembles a king piece
from draughts in its powers).

The game is played with dice and general movement is as in Backgammon, along
intersecting, but not quite opposite, paths.

http://www.chebache.com/basics.html gives better info on the basics,
strangely enough you need to download the Chebache program to get access to
the complete rules.
