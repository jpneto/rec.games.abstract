The game is Eleusis, invented by Abbott in '56, and first described by
Gardner in Scientific American, #200, 1959, pp160-164.
The game was subsequently modified by Abbott (in '77), and called New Eleusis.

I have papers describing this game, but hopefully someone else will
have stuff online.  If not I'll scan the rules and post them.
