By combining the rules of Neutron and Teeko,
I came up with a game that I have described
at the bottom of

http://home.hia.no/~jkhaug00/games.htm


An idea: How about making it illegal to put
the last piece you moved back to the same
square you moved it from in your last move?
This is an attempt to avoid "cowardish" play
where one player takes a piece back and
forth to ensure 1/2 point against an assumed
stronger player, and the other player is
forced to play so to avoid a quick loss.
This makes it harder to analyze because the
set of legal moves is not given by the
position alone, so I am wondering if anyone
could help me check how this rule would
affect the number of draws and the maximum
length of perfect (not drawn) games. Thanks!
