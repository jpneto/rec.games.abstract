These names are actually somewhat meaningful in Dutch and/or German
languages. (I don't really know either much beyond looking in
dictionaries)  Gipf comes from gipfen meaning summit.  Tamsk I couldn't

has connotations of breaking up, as in removing zertz hexagons to break
up the board into pieces.
