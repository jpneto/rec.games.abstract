Here are some proverbs gleaned from past discussions about this perennial:


"Strategy is what you want... tactics is what you get."

------------

 "Tactics is about doing things right,
  strategy is about doing the right things."

------------

   "Tactics is knowing what to do when there is something to do,
    strategy is knowing what to do when there is  nothing to do."

------------

Strategy is when I don't know what I'm doing;
       tactics is when I know what I'm not doing.

------------
