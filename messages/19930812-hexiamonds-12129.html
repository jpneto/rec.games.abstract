Hexiamonds:
-----------
There are 12 hexiamonds. Has anyone made figures from two sets of hexiamonds?

I constructed some figures for the 48 problem for this set.

48-Problem:
Divide the 24 hexiamonds into 3 groups of 8 each. Find a 48-triangle region
that each of the three groups will cover.

The analog 24-Problem for one set of hexiamonds has ca. 50 different regions.
see: Torbijn, Polyiamonds, JoRM 2:4 (1969) 216-227
     Philpott, Polyomino and Polyiamond Problems: Part II, JoRM 10:2 (1977) 98
     Gardner, Sixth Book of Math. Games from SA, Chap. 18, (1971)
