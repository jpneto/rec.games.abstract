Well, in effect it still does, alas. It's just precisely that -
with the added rule, that when you play the second of two
diagonally adjacent pieces in octocells, you automatically get
played the intervening quadcell piece as well, if it's vacant.

As Quax is just the same, but with the quadcells needing to be
played as a full move, I might humbly suggest the name "QuikQuax"
for games played with this protocol; though 1st-come-1st-connected
is also great, and self-explanatory, though rather long, unless
abbreviated to FCFC which is also snappy (though no longer clear).
