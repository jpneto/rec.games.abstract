I guess in my case, my specialty is a particular class of abstract games which are naturally finite (superko not required) but which do have cycling.  I.e. pieces are added and removed but cannot be recycled indefinitely.  These games include:
  Cephalopod (http://www.marksteeregames.com/Cephalopod_rules.pdf)
  Tanbo (http://www.marksteeregames.com/Tanbo_rules.pdf)
  Rive (http://www.marksteeregames.com/Rive_rules.pdf)
  Oust (http://www.marksteeregames.com/Oust_rules.pdf)

Oust stands out within the class because the board starts out empty and ends with only one color of stones on the board - exceedingly rare.
