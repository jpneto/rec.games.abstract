The rules of Capablanca chess, invented by former world Chess champion
Capablanca:

size of board: 8x10
type of pieces: stand chess pieces + archbishop + chancellor
move of new pieces: archbishop: knight + bishop ; chancellor: knight + rook
other changes: kings moves three squares instead of two when castling
opening configuration (A means archbishop, C means chancellor):

(black)
r n a b q k b c n r
p p p p p p p p p p

(WHITE)

Otherwise identical to normal chess
