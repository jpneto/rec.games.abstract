Games to the Rescue <http://www.ludism.org/scwiki/GamesToTheRescue> is
a philanthropic project of the Center for Ludic Synergy and Seattle
Cosmic Game Night.  The aim of the project is to provide game
equipment and a book of game rules to hospitals, for use by patients
and visitors.

You can help our project in a variety of ways; see the page linked
to above for details.
