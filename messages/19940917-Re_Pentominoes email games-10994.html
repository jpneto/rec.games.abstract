[variations on the alphabetic mnemonics for the 12 pentominoes]


When I was in high school, I made a list of the 35 hexominoes,
and came up with a decent mapping into the 36 alphanumerics.
Some of it was completely arbitrary (N, S, Z, 2, and 5 are all
pretty much interchangable), some required a bit of lookahead
(there's one that looks like a lowercase t, but we'll call it
an X (with a slight tail) instead, since nothing else could
possibly be X, and there's another excellent candidate for an
uppercase T), and some just plain didn't fit anywhere and were
arbitrarily assigned to whatever was leftover.
