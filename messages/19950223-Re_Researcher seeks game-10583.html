Ataxx, I believe is like the computer game Spot. In it, you move your
pieces either one or two spaces.  The move need not be in a straight
line.  If you move one space, the space you move from is still
occupied with one of your pieces.  If you move 2 spaces the old space
is left empty.  Any pieces you land next to change to your color.
   Object is to occupy more spaces than your opponent when you are
done.
