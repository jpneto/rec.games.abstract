That's slightly different. The escort method of capture is used in a number
of games and on hexes too. Four of my games use this capture rule on hexes
(OK - three of them are part of a compendium so that's possibly a bit of a
cheat!) and two, including my version of tables / hnefatafl, use the
capture on squares. see Maldon, Wiggul & HexAgon at:
        http://www.aponline.co.uk
In commonly known games there is a very restricted number of capture
methods whilst plundering history and the imagination can easily produce
dozens. Escort capture is possibly the most common after replacement and
jump and used in games from Japan to England (and Scandinavia of course).
