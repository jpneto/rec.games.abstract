	**** Announcing the first Real-Time Internet Othello Tournament ****

	Date:		Saturday, January 7th
	Time:		4pm-7pm and 8pm-11pm IOS time (see below)
	Place:		Internet Othello Server (IOS)
				(telnet faust.uni-paderborn.de 5000)
	Categories:	- Open (any human may enter)
			- Novice (humans under 1500 or unestablished IOS rating)
	Format:		Six round Swiss-style tournament
	Entry fee:	None
	Entry deadline:	As soon as possible ... must be by 3pm IOS time on
				January 7th
	Prizes:		Eternal Internet Fame
	Time controls:	25 minutes/player/game, standard 2-minute lose-on-time
				procedures as on IOS

To enter, send e-mail to me (springer@math.umn.edu) giving your name, IOS
login, and the division you plan to enter.  Since the two divisions will
run concurrently, you may only enter one division.

Given the effort required in running such a tournament, I will be directing
the tournament, not playing in it.  All players must be logged on to IOS by
3:45pm IOS time, and any players who choose to withdraw during the tournament
will not be welcome in the next IOS real-time tournament.

Tournament Schedule:

Round 1:    t:00 -   t:50
Round 2:  t+1:00 - t+1:50
Round 3:  t+2:00 - t+2:50
 break :  t+3:00 - t+3:50
Round 4:  t+4:00 - t+4:50
Round 5:  t+5:00 - t+5:50
Round 6:  t+6:00 - t+6:50

Where t is:
		- 7 in California
		- 8 in Montana
		- 9 in Minnesota
		- 10 in New York
		- 15 in England
		- 16 in Germany and France
(using a 24 hour clock).

The break after round 3 is expected to be a lunch break in North America and
a dinner break in Europe.  Of course, with such diverse time zones involved, 
these times won't be convenient for all ... I hope they are a good compromise.

I look forward to an enjoyable tournament for all,

Colin Springer -- Tournament Director
