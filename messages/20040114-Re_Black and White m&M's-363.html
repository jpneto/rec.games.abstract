I found no obvious way for either Black or White to force a win.  That
being said, I think it's likely that repeated play would reveal a need
to add a further balancing restriction or two.


Blocking every  4-in-a-row could be achieved with a pattern that covers only
1/4 of the infinite plane.  While the orthogonally-connected rule limits the
ability of a player to achieve this pattern, the fact that a player has
twice as many moves to black as are theoretically necessary makes it very
likely that a player can block.  Hence you (probably) won't have any way for
a player to force a win.
