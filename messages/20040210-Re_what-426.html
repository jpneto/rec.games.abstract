Abstract games are in a sense games without a strong theme or
setting.  Examples of abstract games are Chess and Go.  Games
such as Monopoly, Settlers of Catan and Squad Leader are not.


While it's not part of the definition of "abstract game", most 
discussions here refer to two-player games of perfect information with 
no chance elements.  Often ones with rather simple rules compared to 
(say) chess.
