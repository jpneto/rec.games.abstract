SPOILER:
Solution to Problem 4:
H should play D5!  If V blocks at E5, then C2 B2 C3 B4 B6 follow.
There are other tricky lines, but they basically all use the same
moves in other orders.  (Other winning first moves include C2, E5,
and F3, I think, but the winning idea is the same, with D5 coming
a move or two down the line.)
