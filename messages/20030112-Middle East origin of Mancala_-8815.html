Mancala is generally thought to have originated in Africa.  However, a letter
in the new issue of Biblical Archaeology Review (Jan/Feb 2003) claims that an
artifact found in Jericho and dating to the 7th millennium B.C.E. points to the
game's origin in ancient Israel.  Would anyone with a knowledge of Mancala's
history like to comment on this possibility?
