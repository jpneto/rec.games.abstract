The notation will be:
	<pos>[-<pos>],<pos>

Where <pos> is:  <Row><Col>

The 1st position specified is pushed to the position after the comma.

For example,

         1 2 3 4 5
        / / / / / 6
    A- . . . . . / 7
   B- . . . . . . / 8
  C- . . . . . . . / 9
 D- . . . . . . . . /
E- . . . . . . . . .
 F- . 1 1 1 2 2 . .
  G- . . . . . . .
   H- . . . . . .
    I- . . . . .

         1 2 3 4 5
        / / / / / 6
    A- . . . . . / 7
   B- . . . . . . / 8
  C- . . . . . . . / 9
