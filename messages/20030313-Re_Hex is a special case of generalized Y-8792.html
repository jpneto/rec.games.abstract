Hex and Y enthusiasts may wish to check out Jack van Ryswyck's paper,
"Search and Evaluation in Hex," available for download in the research
section of his homepage (http://www.cs.ualberta.ca/~javhar/).  The
paper discusses various approaches to programming AI for Hex and Y,
and it also describes some interesting mathematical properties of Y
(and therefore Hex as well.)
