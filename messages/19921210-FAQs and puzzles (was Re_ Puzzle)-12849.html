I know ya'll know this, but nobody's said it this week for newbies:
Rec.games.abstract is for the discussion of games without hidden or
random factors, commonly described as games which take "a moment to
learn, a lifetime to master."  These would include games like chess,
othello(reversi), noughts and crosses, checkers, TwiXT, mancala and
variants, and many others.  Some of these have their own newsgroups.
In an abstract game, each player has equal information (ie complete)
and there are no dice or other random elements.  Pure strategy. . .
