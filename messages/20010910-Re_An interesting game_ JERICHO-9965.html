This would
cause situations where three or more columns reside on one line. How

If you have two columns on a line on your side of the board, only the
strongest one is compared with the strongest one on the same line on the
opponent's side of the board.

The goal is to have seven wins simultaneously. There are 13 contended lines,
so the the game is won when seven lines are conquered.
