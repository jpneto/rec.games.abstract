I'm curious about the relative values of opening moves. There are two determining characteristics, the largest group and the number of groups. Both count fairly heavily. Say you've got a largest ten-group and also ten groups, then adding to the ten-group and starting a new (own) group result in the same increase. Starting a new group matters little in terms of where you place it. Positional considerations aside, the might be placed anywhere and count the same. So determining the relative value of opening moves isn't immediately clear to me - and the use of a pie depends on precisely that.
