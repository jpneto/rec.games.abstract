I'd like to know about funny games (card games or games where only paper
and pen is needed) where only two players are required. Name of the game,

Mastermind and Connect-Four are examples of good 2-player games
sold with props, where pencil and paper would be almost as good.

Clue (the classic Parker Bros game) can be played with no props except
a deck of cards (forget the dice, and moving from room to room,
which make the game less fun anyway).

Another great pencil and paper game (though a dictionary may be needed)
is Twentyfive Boxes which I reviewed some years ago in this
newsgroup and/or rec.puzzles.
