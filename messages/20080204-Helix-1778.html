The three toroidal games announced here recently:

1. Torben's Torex.  Hexagonal grid.  Draws are possible.

2. Bill Taylor's NZ.  Rectangular grid.  Needs Crossway rule to
prevent draws.

3. Mark Steere's Helix.  Hexagonal grid.  Filled board produces
exactly one winner.
