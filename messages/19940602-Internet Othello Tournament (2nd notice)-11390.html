This is the final announcement about the Second Internet Othello Competition.
In order to compete in all rounds you must register by this Sunday (June 5th),
although late entrants will be accepted (you'll just have to miss the first
round or so).

For information on the tournament, log on to the IOS (Internet Othello Server):

telnet faust.uni-paderborn.de 5000

When you're on, type "tournament" for details, and how to register.  Otherwise,
just mail me directly and I can mail you the information.
