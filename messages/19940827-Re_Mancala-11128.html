I recall a version of Mancala called Owari, or something like
it.  The Kallahs were just to hold won pieces (in fact there was
a round board with twelve holes).  I do not recall all the 
rules, but I believe the object was to land on a square with 2 or 3
pieces counting yours.  Then you captured all consecutive previous 
squares with 2 or 3 pieces.  Perhaps somebody can find the reference
by that name.
 
One other detail.  I have heard it said that in the West Indies some
people claim to be able to determine a man's African tribal ancestry
by which version of Mancala he plays.
