It occurs to me that folks here might not be aware of Star Chess (
http://www.kynosarges.de/StarChess.html ). Because of its name, it is
sometimes discussed in chess ngs, even though it is not a form of chess.

It is an interesting 4-person pure abstract game. The AI is weak; after a
few games it becomes far too easy to beat it. However when 4 humans play,
the game can test your negotiating and algebra-in-your-head skills. The
players set up diagonally from each other tend to become de facto allies,
even when no explicit negotiation is done.
