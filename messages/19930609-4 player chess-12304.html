Some of my friends came up with a version of 4-player chess some time ago.
The game is played on one board, with each player having half of one
colors pieces, starting at opposing corners.

The board looked something like this:

	WQ WP>      BR BN BB BK
	WB WP>      BP BP BP BP
	WN WP>       v  v  v  v
	WR WP>
			 <WP WR
	 ^  ^  ^  ^	 <WP WN
	BP BP BP BP	 <WP WB
	BQ BB BN BR	 <WP WK

The arrows show direction of movement.
Play starts with White Queen and proceeds counter-clockwise. (WQ-BQ-WK-BK)
The two players of a color are on the same side, but may not discuss their
moves (no table talk).
  
The losing King is checkmated on his turn, not when the mating piece is put
into position.  The Queen may block/capture the offending piece, or may even
checkmate the other King first.

This makes for fairly quick games, and the (optional) no talking rule
combines well with speed to make interesting games.
