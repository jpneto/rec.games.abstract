If your question is "does the board change completely from turn to
turn or can you plan ahead?" then the answer is "no, it's not that
chaotic, and yes, you can definitely plan ahead." The crucial point
which is not easy to see from just reading the rules is that rings
cannot be jumped, so you can use these as blockades and keep your
little dots from getting reversed from every which way. It's
reminiscent of other games in the series in that you can see what you
want to do and you can see what you want to prevent, and yet sometimes
the clever move will suddenly appear and delight all and sundry.
