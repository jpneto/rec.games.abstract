By pure twist of fate, I happened upon this game in "Exploring Mathematics
With Your Computer", by Arthur Engel, page 169.  It is called "Wythoff's

Yes! Beatty's Theorem says that if a and b are irrational numbers
greater than 1 satisfying 1/a + 1/b = 1, then the set of positive
integers is a disjoint union of {[an]}_(n >= 1) and {[bn]}_(n >= 1).

since the last time I encountered it...
