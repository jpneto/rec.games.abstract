Many good games are derivative, but make crucial (but sometimes simple)
changes that make the new games interesting in their own right.  So
there is nothing wrong with being derivative, as long as the derivative
gives sufficiently different play from the original to make it
worthwhile to play both.

For example, Blokus Trigon is clearly derivative of Blokus, but the
change to a triangular grid changes gameplay enough that it is
interesting to play both versions.
