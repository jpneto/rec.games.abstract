Complete rules for Mancala and several varients can be found in
_Board and Table Games from Many Civilizations_, by R. C. Bell.
The publisher is Dover Publications, and the ISBN is 0-486-23855-5.
While not all of the games listed are "abstract" in the sense defined
by the charter of this group, it's a lovely book to while away a rainy
day.

Enjoy...
