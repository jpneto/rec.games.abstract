The 1975 book "Mudcrack Y and Poly Y" by Craige Schensted & Charles Titus
has the rules for "Poly-Join" ("Join" being their name for standard Hex)
which is played on a board with 4x goals. The rules are identical to Atoll
with different win criteria. They interpreted the object of Hex (Join)
differently. Rather than "connect your goals" or "connect the opposite sides
of the board", they saw it as joining goals into as few groups as possible.
On a standard Hex board, there is no difference. On an 8 or more sided
poly-join board it isn't.
