I hope he will forgive me for putting words in his mouth. In the US,
we (well, the societal we, certainly not me) spend an inordinate

syllabus, the children need to answer 50 randomly chosen
multiplication problems (1 digit by 1 digit) in 5 minutes in order to
move on to the next phase of the syllabus (have to master learning
objective n before you can move on to totally unrelated objective
n+1). They are tested on their ability to multiply 2 and 3 digit
numbers by hand.
