Boardspace.net is a site to play abstract board games in real time,
against real people or robots.   Yinsh (project Gipf game 5) is
the newest addition to the site.   In all, four games are
available:  Yinsh Plateau Zertz and Lines of Action.

Boardspace is free and has no advertising.
