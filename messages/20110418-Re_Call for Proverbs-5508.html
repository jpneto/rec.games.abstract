"I have looked through your game, and I find but little in it. At one
time, played by gentlemen, it might have been worth practising; but it

your equal, in three months more I could give you a pawn; but I
renounce the pursuit, and bid you farewell. I shall never play
draughts again!" -Alexandre Louis Honore Lebreton Deschapelles

"A player can win only as a consequence of an error made by the
