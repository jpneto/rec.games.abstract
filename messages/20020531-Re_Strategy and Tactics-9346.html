I have to disagree with your opinion on reversi/othello.
It is extremely difficult to visualize future board positions
and hence you generally can't play tactically and must play
almost purely strategically.  I would put the strategy % up
around 90.

With hex, it depends on the board size and unfortunately, there
is no standard size.  Larger boards are more strategic and
smaller boards are more tactical.  Your percentages seem about
right for say a 14x14 board.  For a 11x11 board, the strategy %
drops to maybe 30 (IMO).
