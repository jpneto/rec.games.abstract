**What is ACCESS.bus?**   ACCESS.bus is an industry standard desktop bus for
the connectivity of computer peripherals.  It is a simple and inexpensive
method of linking multiple low-speed input/output devices to a single port
of a personal computer, workstation, or terminal.  ACCESS.bus devices
include keyboards, mice, trackballs, joysticks, scanners, digitizers, tablets,
light pens, virtual reality gloves, audio/visual interfaces, peripherals for
the disabled, and printers and modems.
