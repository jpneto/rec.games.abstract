What tradeoff do you want between showing intermediate ability in
many games versus mastery of one or two?


I just thought that the spirit of a pentathlon is to show overall strength and
well-roundedness as an abstract gamer. In the Olympics, the winner of the
decathlon probably didn't win each individual event as well. Likewise, the
winners of the individual events may not have done so well in events at which
they were not as good. For example, marathon runners are usually not the best
at shot put. Likewise, a chess master may lose at renju.
