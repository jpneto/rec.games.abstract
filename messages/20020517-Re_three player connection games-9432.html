(like Hex, or Bridge-it) for three (or more) players.

probably be necessary to add a third dimensional element
to guarantee a game had this condition in an `interesting'
case. I have a gut feeling the question applied to a two
dimensional game has a connection to the four colour
problem, but I can't quite put my finger on how.
