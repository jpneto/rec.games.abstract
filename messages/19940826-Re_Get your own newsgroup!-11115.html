I have to agree with Andy. The whole idea of having a specific newsgroup is
being undermined here. I find much of the checkers postings to be interesting,
but that's not the point. I find ZEN buddhism interesting, too, but I'm
not about to start a discussion about it, unless there's some relevance to
chess in the discussion. Now, some of the checkers postings do have relevance
to chess, but many do not. It has gotten to the point where the postings
about checkers, postings protesting the postings on checkers, and postings
trying to show the relevance of checkers to rec.games.chess, and postings
about GO vs. chess, far outnumber the postings about chess. I'm ready to
start a newsgroup rec.only.chess. I think that rec.games.abstract is the
perfect place to post both checkers and chess postings. In fact, I hope
that the parties involved will read this in rec.games.abstract; because I'm
not going to post it to rec.games.chess.
