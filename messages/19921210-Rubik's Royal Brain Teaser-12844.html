We bought Rubik's Royal Brain Teaser.  I don't know if it's old, so I
won't bother describing it other than saying that it is a jigsaw
puzzle with a twist.

Anyway, two of us put the puzzle together in a couple of hours.  But
the teaser on the box implies that we were supposed to solve it in a
more deductive way.  Two questions that we never figured out are:

How can you predict the orientation of the corner jewels?
Do the white jewels fall randomly and do they introduce randomness 
or can their appearance and behavior be predicted?
