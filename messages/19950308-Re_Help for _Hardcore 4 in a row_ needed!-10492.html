There's a game called Qubic; it's 4-in-a-row on a 4x4x4 board.
Qubic has been proved to be a first-player win by L. Victor Allis
(see his PhD thesis, which is available by anonymous FTP from
ftp.cs.vu.nl in /pub/victor).
