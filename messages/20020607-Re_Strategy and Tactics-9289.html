You're the one who (earlier) said that sound sacrifices always recover thier
material. No. Sound sacrifices always help attain the ultimate objective (a win
or draw). Sometimes that's by regaining material, sometimes it's not.
"[M]aterial equality" is not the point. Material is just the most obvious
'positional' consideration, not something apart from positional chess. A
sacrifice either improves your position or it doesn't.
