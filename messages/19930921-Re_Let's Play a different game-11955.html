Grey: I'm attacking Northern Europe from Great Britain with 7 armies.

Black: Plays stone that assures the formation of a second eye.

Tangerine: Inserts Tab A in Slot B for the third time, thus ensuring
          that White spends his next turn in the Cathedral.

White: Damn. Where did I put that "Get out of Cathedral Free" card?
      Oh well. I pass my marbles to the left, and tag Grey. "You're it!"

Grey rolls a seven and fumbles. 3 armies are drowned in the Channel
    and one turn is lost.

Grey: I use my temporary "it" powers to reinstate the lost turn.  I then
     propose an alliance with White in return for using my one-shot "it"
     crowning to annoint White Pope.

White: You have my blessing, Grey. I hereby issue a Papal Bull declaring
     Crimson to be a heretic. Let's see you worm your way out of that one!

Crimson: A heretic I may be, but the word was "pneumonoultramicrosilicavolcan-
       ocaniosis", so White loses that round of Hangman, and becomes Pope-
       On-A-Rope. I jump Grey and Tangerine, and reach the end of the board.
       King me.

Well, this one's all over but the shouting, Crimson has fallen for Black's
brilliant trap. The recently created Crimson King is clearly mated in 2.
Of the remaining players, Lemon-blue is the only one left with a timeout.
With no reason to use it, Lemon-blue can redeem it for a chance to open the
door to their "Mystery Date" -- good luck.

Lemon-blue: I open the third door without looking in, and stands silently
behind it. When Black looks behind the door, I shout 'Numerical Estimation',
