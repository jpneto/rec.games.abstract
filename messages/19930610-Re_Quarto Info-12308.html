GAME
	Quarto!

BACKGROUND

	The game has won the award 'Best Toy of the Year in All Categories'
	at some toy-happening in Cannes, France.
	[more details anyone? -neilb]

RULES

	Quarto! is a two-player game, played on a board of four by four
	squares, and with sixteen pieces, all different. A piece has the
	following characteristics:

		- White or black
		- Big or small
		- Round or square
		- Whole, or with a hole in the top

	So, you have a small black square with a hole, a small black square
	that is whole, a big black square with a hole, etc, etc.

	One player begins, and he selects a piece. His opponent must place
	the piece on the board, and select a piece to give back, and so
	on.  The winner of the game is the player that completes a line of
	four.  A line of four consists of four pieces, that have (at least)
	one thing in common, be it that they are all black, or all big, or
	whatever.  A line of four can be vertical, horizontal or diagonal.

	So, a line of four can consist of:
		- A big white round with a hole
		- A big black square that is whole,
		- A big white square with a hole,
		- A big white round that is whole.
	Here all four pieces in the line are big.

	It's easy to learn, but not so easy to master.
