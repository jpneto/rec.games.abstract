Just a quick question about 7th guest to all the strategists out there:

There is a brain teaser on 7th Guest that has been hounding me *forever*...
.... please help me solve it!  The situation is this:  Before you lies a 
chessboard.  Every time you click on a square, a queen chesspiece arises.  
The object of the puzzle is to place eight queens on the board, with no two 
aligned either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.... If two queens 
are placed in a line, one will disappear. 

I've been able to get 7 queens on there, but never the 8th.  Can anyone 
help me?

-- Milt



Milt,

try the following setup:

  abcdefgh
8  . . .q. 
7 . . q . 
6  .q. . .
5 q . . . 
4  . . q .
3 . . . .q
2  q . . .
1 . .q. . 

ie. queens on a5, b2, c6, d1, e7, f4, g8 and h3.
