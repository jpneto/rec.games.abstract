I also don't know of this restriction. I played Hex some time ago very often and
I never saw an advantage for the first player in starting in the middle.
Yes, it's the best move to begin in my opinion, but there are also some
standard moves to defend this beginning. So I think that Hex is a very good
balanced game (surely on boards 9x9 and wider) and that there is no need of
restricting the first move.
