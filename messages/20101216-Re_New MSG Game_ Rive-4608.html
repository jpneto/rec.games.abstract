Rive is tactical as opposed tp strategical because there are no
"caclulable signposts along the way": everything is changeable. It
grows at a monks pace, but it grows, so it is finite. The number of
cells is odd, so someone will win.
Obviously, because of these characteristics, it will fit only small
boards. Being at a loss about the reason for winning or losing it
comes with the territory. Play on small boards should eventually
unveil strategy (tactical games _do_ have strategy of course).
