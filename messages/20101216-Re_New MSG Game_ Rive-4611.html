Ok, try this:

"STONE PLACEMENT A group here is comprised of interconnected stones of both
colors. If you can place a stone in isolation, you must do so. Otherwise
your stone must be placed adjacent to up to three groups, of which the
largest must be as small as possible."

In the example you provided, Christian, the largest group connecting to the
newly added stone cannot possibly be smaller than two, so any cell which
connects to groups no larger than two is legal.  Since all of the empty
cells in this case meet that requirement, all of the empty cells are legal.
