Here's a great idea for a game: play Master Mind with two players guessing
and a third player (which can easily be a computer) telling the two players
what the results of their guesses is. First player to guess it wins.


Another interesting variant is to write a computer player that does
not commit to a color assignment, but dynamically chooses answers to
be both consistent with its past answers and to minimize the
information content of the past answers -- to play the optimal
opponent.
