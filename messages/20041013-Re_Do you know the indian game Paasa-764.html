What you describe sounds vaguely like Chausar (also known as Chaupar
and Chaupad), which is a variant of Pachisi played with long dice.
The traditional Chausar / Pachisi board is made of cloth, but "pasa"
used in the generic sense could refer to any game played with
long-dice. Here are a couple of links for you:
