In <1994Jun29.115800.7984@Princeton.EDU> akgee@flagstaff.Princeton.EDU (Malkav's daughter) writes:


Does anyone out there know where I can find extra accessories and rules for
multiplayer (i.e. more than 2) Abalone?  Also, if anyone has played Abalone
with more than two players, how does it play?


Yes. The game with 3 players is simmilar to the normal 2-player game.

except:

- there are three start-positions at three lines from the hexagon. each
player has 3 marbles (?) less than in the 2-player game.

    X X . G G
   X X . . G G
  X X . . . G G
 X X . . . . G G
X X . . . . . G G
 X . . . . . . G
  . . . . . . .				
   O O O O O O
    O O O O O
					... that's it


- the first player, who pushed out 5 marbles from the two other players,
is winner.

- there is only one possible other situation as in the 2-player game:

-> -> -> ..XXXOG.. -> -> ->

in this Situation it is possible to push two colors!

but in this Situation

-> -> -> ..XXXOX.. -> -> ->

or in this

-> -> -> ..XXXOOG.. -> -> ->

it is IMpossible to push.

...hope this helps (sorry about my bad english :-( )
