master level games would almost certainly increase all the same, if one-
move equalization is used. Do you propose other rule changes to reduce the

If the swap rule were implemented, what would a fair opening move be, knowing
that Black  now has the option to swap it?  Certainly not 1.e4 or 1.d4.
1.Nc3?  1.b4?  1.a3?  What would you play tomorrow, as White, if the tourney
you entered adopted the swap rule?


These openings have been tried and largely abandoned by strong players because
they are too drawish under standard rules. Maybe 5-move equalization would
stir up the pot better. Which side of 1.e4 2.e5 3.Nf3 4.f5 5.Bc4 would you
want to be on?
