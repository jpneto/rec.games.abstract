Do Othello programs use features measuring general stability, ie the stability
of interior discs?  Most programs do some checking for stable configurations
of edge pieces, but I don't know of any that calculate interior stability.  
I am aware that Rosenbloom's IAGO used an interior stability feature.  It
sounds like it was pretty expensive, and he concluded that because of the
approximation involved (IAGO scanned the axes of each piece) it was only
marginally useful.  

Is anyone aware of any (good) Othello program that measures internal
stability?
