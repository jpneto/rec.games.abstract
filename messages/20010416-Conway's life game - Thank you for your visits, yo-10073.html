Conway's game of life purpose:
On a grid, you fill several cells.
Then you run the computation.
Step by step, view cells multiply each others, die, according to very simple
rules.
Evolution is very unpredictable after 3 or 4 generations.
Very special configurations as "spaceships" give very special results!
