Yes, except that there are no stacks in this game. :) I guess you meant groups.

Before releasing the game, I checked several positions of the type you mention, but all of them were finite, and I thought, apparently rather naively, that there weren't any other relevant types of patterns to look into. PCM's clever idea was combining two such first type positions in a single one such that the groups in one of them affected the distances in the other, and viceversa. But, even with that idea in mind, finding a working cycle must have been the hardest part.
