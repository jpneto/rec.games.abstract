	The BITNET listserv has an archive with records of shogi games.
Most are uncommented, but a few have annotations. To get a list of the
files available, send mail to listserv@technion.technion.ac.il or 
listserv@technion.bitnet with the body
GET SHOGI-L FILELIST

	You retrieve the files with the same command; e. g. 
GET OI MATCH-92
to get the newly-added listing of the games in the 1902 match for
the Oi title.
