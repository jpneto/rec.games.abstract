For those who don't know, Quick Chess is played on a 5x6 board.
No castling, no initial 2-space pawn moves allowed.  The initial
setup looks like this:

        6 r b k q n
        5 p p p p p
        4 - - - - -
        3 - - - - -
        2 P P P P P
        1 R B K Q N
          a b c d e

Any decent program should be quite strong at this.
