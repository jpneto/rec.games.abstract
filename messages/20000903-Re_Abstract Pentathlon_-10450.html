I think a pentathalon should include game that require original
strategy rather than mastery of existing theory and openings.  (Others
with different tastes may well disagree, of course.)

1. Quoridor
2. Octi
3. Twixt
4. Cathedral
5. Gipf

All these games have different mechanics and objectives.  Quoridor is a
racing game.  Octi is a game of territorial conquest, where players
create their own pieces.  Twixt is a connection game.  Cathedral is a
crowding game.  Gipf is a distant cousing of Abalone, but is
strategically deeper and less prone to stalemate.
