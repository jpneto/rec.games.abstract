Also try Pilade http://faidutti.free.fr/jeux/autres/pilade.html
Game of strategy for two players, by Bruno Faidutti
Rules of the game :
Place the 12 discs flat on the table between the two players. Before you are
now 12 stacks of discs, with each stack being a single disc tall.
Each player takes a turn by taking an entire stack and placing it on top of
another stack sharing a common characteristic.
There are 2 characteristics:
a) The two stacks are the same height.
b) The two stacks are both topped with the same color disc.
A player must move the entire stack-dividing a stack is not allowed. The
game ends when one player has no legal move. That player loses.
