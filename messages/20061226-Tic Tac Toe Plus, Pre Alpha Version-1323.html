The pre-alpha version of Tic Tac Toe Plus, which is actually a Tic Tac
Toe game, will be made available on Wed 27th.

Your contribution is needed and will be appreciated as we would like
persons to sample this version of the game and give their honest
comments, whether good or bad, report errors and problems, or give any
good idea that will be splendid for the game.

This version is only a pre-alpha version, it is not a completed game,
therefore do not expect mush visuals, options or gameplay as a matter
of fact.

To test the pre-alpha version, subscribe to the group "Makers Of Video
Games" at http://groups.google.com.vc/group/maker-of-video-games?hl=en.
When your membership is permitted, which should not take any longer
than 6 hours, you can then download and play the game.

Give all comments, reports, ideas and ask any question in the topic
labelled "Comments on Tic Tac Toe Plus, Pre Alpha Version" at the group
"Makers Of Video Games."

Please read the Page labelled "Tic Tac Toe Plus, Pre Alpha Version" in
the group "Makers Of Video Games" before downloading and playing.
