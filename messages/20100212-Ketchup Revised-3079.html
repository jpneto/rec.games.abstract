After a much more play testing, Ketchup has been changed a bit.  Now
the players add 2 and 3 stones respectively on their turns, and one
rule that turned out to be unnecessary has been removed.  I can't say
that the rules won't ever change again (the necessity of a pie rule
remains unclear, and I still haven't gotten to test it on square
boards), but I'm very happy with it even as it is now.
