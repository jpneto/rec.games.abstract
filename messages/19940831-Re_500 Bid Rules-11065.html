Does anyone have a FAQ or set of rules to 500 BID?  The reason I ask is that

Practically every card game that's evolved over time (which
I guess includes practically all card games) tend to have lots 
and lots of variants, which may be regional.  I swear every time
I look up a game in Hoyle, it's different in at least one
or two ways from the version I know.

In the case of 500 (I've always just called it "500" as 
opposed to "500 bid"), I originally learned to use inkling
and a 5-card widow from my parents.  We also use the
nello (spelling?) and double nello bids, but I've played 
with people who didn't.  I also originally learned to play
using a standard auction bid (like bridge), but then my
parents changed their story and started playing with 
only 1 bid per player (which I don't like nearly as much).

So I don't have any firm answers for you, but I would guess
that these are just all regional variations to the game...
