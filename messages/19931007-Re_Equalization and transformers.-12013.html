Nifty chess alteration. Player one plays a legal white move, a legal 
black move and another legal white move. Player two then plays a legal
black move, a legal white move, and a legal black move. etc.
No new rules are needed, and the objective doesn't need to be changed.
And I can't think of any game to which this technique cannot be generalized.
