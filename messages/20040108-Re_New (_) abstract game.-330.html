Yes, when we play the rectangular version we allow diagonals.
It definitely spices things up, otherwise hex and quad would be too similar.



The first player draws any legal move, and subsequent moves are allowed
to continue from either "free end", ending when both ends are trapped. 


Hey!  Never thought of that; (though we should've - our email diagrams look
very "unfinished" with one "loose end"!) That sounds like a very good idea,
and I hope Joao and Richard might agree.  It also makes the game more
naturally CGT-like, as there is a natural "addition of games", WW style.

And it leads to the obvious extension that there might even be more than
one such snake/chain, with 2n live ends for n snakes.  *VERY* CGT!


We are still open to nice names for naming this nameless game.

I feel the hex and quad versions should have related names.

Any suggestions from the crowd?
