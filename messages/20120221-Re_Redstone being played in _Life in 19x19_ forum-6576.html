And I started to wonder.  What the fuck did Bill Taylor ever do to

The answer boiled down to Taylor's constantly hyped "credential" - his
teaching certificate.  Lots of people have teaching certificates.
Torben Mogensen has a teaching certificate, and he too knows fuck all
about abstract game design.

Everyone was paying homage to this fat fuck, Bill Taylor, sitting on a

stop taking up space.   But no matter how I tried, I couldn't pry him
