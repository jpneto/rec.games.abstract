Subject: 2002 Nordic Othello Championship in Oslo June 8-9th
From: "Vidar Aas" <vidaas@start.no>
Date: 30/04/2002, 17:18
Newsgroups: rec.games.abstract,rec.games.board

The Norwegian Othello Federation welcomes Othello (Reversi) players from
all the Nordic countries to compete for the title of Nordic Othello
Champion 2002 in Oslo June 8-9th.  There will also be a team
championship.

This is an open tournament for players of all levels.  These
championships are always very friendly, so why not make the trip to Oslo
this weekend?

Further information at http://home.no.net/nof, or contact Tor Birger
Skogen, e-mailaddress tbskogen@mil.no.
