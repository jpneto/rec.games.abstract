A game for four or more players.

Start with three pennies in front of each player.
Each in turn removes from the game one of the pennies.
When a player has no pennies left he is out, and ceases to act.
The last player with a penny left wins.

This can be played with two players, and is then trivial;  or with
three, and is then almost trivial.  But with more than three it becomes
hard to analyse.
