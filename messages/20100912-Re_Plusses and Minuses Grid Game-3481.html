I played this game a couple more times with someone else. I suggest
that, before the second part of the game, you write down your
prediction lists -- Player 1's left to right along the bottom of the
grid; Player 2's bottom to top along the left side of the grid -- so
that each sign matches up with the respective line of the grid that
divides any two rows/columns in question.

Also, a variation: If the two players choose to do this variation, and
if you are writing the prediction lists along the grid, then the
players are allowed to construct their prediction lists in any order
(not just left to right or bottom to top).
