In a more serious vein -- some people (including me) were sort of
exploring how one might run a "Speakerless Nomic".  It'd be worth
trying to hash out an initial Ruleset for such a beasty.


In our Nomic (Gnomic-look at my WWW-Page, to hear more about it. but be
carefull, our language is German) we have a mix of speaker and
speakerless ruleset. But it`s not initial, because our initial ruleset
was the empty set :-)

Now about the mix:

In our Nomic we have Offices. Each Officekeeper is a normal player,
which can vote, etc. One of the officekeepers do something of the job
from the speaker of agora.




Two issues remain:



 * How are rules ordered?  The standard ruleset uses rule numbers to
   determine precedence, but without a speaker, it's not obvious how
   to number the rules.


we have No order in our rules! the rules are refered over their titles.


 * Why play?  In what I'm familiar with, the "prize" for winning the
   game is becoming Speaker.  What's the prize in a speakerless
   Nomic?  Or are we back to only a single game?


in the moment there is no possibility to win Gnomic. The goal is to play
the one and only game so long as possible. but, there are some players
(a minority!), who want to invoke(?) a victory.
