I do not have a FAQ or tutorial, just a very useful method my dad
taught me when I was a kid.  To make a wooden chess board.
...Cut some strips of both color wood.
...Glue them together, alternating colors.
...Cut that into strips (at a right angle of course).
...Stagger those strips and glue them together.
That is all.
