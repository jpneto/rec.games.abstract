While reading about some Chess and Draughts variations, I 
thought about the possibility of extending draughts with 
the following rule: 

A player is also allowed to move one of his/her pieces one space 
diagonally forward when the position is occupied by one of his
pieces, provided neither of them is a king. The piece at the 
destination square will then automatically become a king.

Has anybody ever heard about this variation anywhere? What are
your opinions about the playability of the resulting game?
