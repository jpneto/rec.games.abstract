I'm not sure what you mean by declative gaming for certain, but you may
be surprised to find out that this may not the "wholly new" concept you
took it for.  To wit: the Nomic games.

Nomic is a game where a very small set of rules is given to start, and
most are "mutable" --- the players can then work to alter the rules and
make up their own within the granted stucture (including the alteration
of that stucture itself).
There are other, related games (such as Senate), but Nomic has the
largest following in the net (it even has Nomic World, a telnet based
server for the game)

Is this the sort of thing you meant?
