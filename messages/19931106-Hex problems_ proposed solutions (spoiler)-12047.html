Here are my proposed solutions to the Hex problems posted by Bert
Enderton. The moves are labeled <move #>. <V move> <H move> etc.
* indicates the key move.
Please note: The "columns" are tilted, so for example the key move
in problem 1 is C2, not D2.

1: Horizontal (H) to play and win.

      7 - - - - - - - 
     6 - - - - - - -        4. ... C2. IF 5. C3 E1 6. A3 B1.
    5 - V - - V - -                    IF 5. D2 C3 6. E3 E4 7. G3 F3.
   4 - V H H - - -
  3 - - - V - - -
 2 - - * - - H -
1 - - - - - - -
 A B C D E F G

2: Vertical (V) to play and win. 

      7 - - - - - - - 
     6 - V H - - - -     5. E2. IF 5. ... E5 6. C3.
    5 - V H - - - -             IF 5. ... C3 6. E5 D7 7. F6.
   4 - V H V - - -
  3 - - - - - - -
 2 - - H - * - -
1 - - - - - - -
 A B C D E F G

3: H to play and win

      7 - - - - - - -
     6 - * - - - H -     5. ... B6. IF 6. B5 B4 7. E3 E4 8. F4 E6
    5 - - V V - V -                    9. E5 C7.
   4 - - H H - - -
  3 - V - - - V -
 2 - - - - - H -
1 - - - - - - -
 A B C D E F G

4: H to play and win

      7 - - - - - - - 
     6 - - - V - - -     6. ... C4. IF 7. D2 E5 8. D5 F3 9. G3 F4
    5 - - - - - - -                    10. G4 F6 11. A5 B6. 
   4 - - * H V - -
  3 - V - - H - -
 2 - - - - V V V
1 - - - - H H H
 A B C D E F G
