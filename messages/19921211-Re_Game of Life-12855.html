The game of Life is an abstract mathematical modell. You play it on a grid of
various size.

There are living cells and dead cells on the grid.Each cell has 8 neighbours.
Wether a cell is alive or dead at the next turn depends on how many neighbours
are alive.

1) If there is no or only one living neighbour, the cell dies.
2) If there are two living neighbours nothing happens.
3) A cell with exact 3 living neighbours will live at the next turn.
4) If there are 4 or more living neighbours, the cell will die.

Examples:
