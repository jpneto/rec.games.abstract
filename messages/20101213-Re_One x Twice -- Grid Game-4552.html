Start with an n-by-n grid drawn on paper.


More generally, let it be a n_odd-by-n_even rectangle
where the player seeking Odd total chooses from {1, ..., n_odd}
and player seeking Even chooses from {1, ..., n_even}.

On 2x2 grid, Odd plays 1,1,2,1 and always wins.

On 3x2 grid, Odd plays 3,3,1,1,2,1 and always wins.

On 2x3 grid, Even plays 1,2,1,3,1 and always wins.

There.  I've solved for three cases.
