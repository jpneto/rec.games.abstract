There is already a Nomic world running.  Taken from "help" on NomicWorld:

	Nomic World is run on a cut-down MUD server to provide a real-time
	multi-person interface by which discussion and proposals for nomic
	may take place.
 
There is an associated mailing list.  To be added, send email to:

	nomic-request@yoyo.cs.monash.edu.au

Messages are sent to

	nomic@yoyo.cs.monash.edu.au

To connect to NomicWorld, use:

	telnet 130.194.67.16 5000

I believe there has been some discussion about email support for
those who don't have internet connectivity, or can't use telnet
for non-standard ports.
