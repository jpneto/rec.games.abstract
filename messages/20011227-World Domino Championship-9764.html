This is last call for the 7th decentralized World Domino Championship, held by
F�d�ration Internationale des Dominoes (FIDO) on Saturday December 29th 2001!

All you have to do is invite four friends or relatives and play exactly three
rounds of dominoes with a double twelve set, aiming for multiples of seven.
This game have similarities to All Fives or Muggins, but is much more
interesting! See http://www.dominospiel.de/fido/indexe.htm for rules and
further information.

Your results should be sent by internet or fax by 24.00 hours German time
(15.00 hours PST) on December 29th. The results will be published the next day,
but there are no prizes.  This is all for fun!
   
Last year there was 60 entrants from Germany, Switzerland, Norway, Estonia and
Iceland.  Join us this year!  

Unfortunately, it's difficult to get double twelve sets (and higher!) if you're
living outside the US, but here are a few recommended places where you can
order some nice sets and participate in next years' world championship:

Puremco (US): http://www.dominoes.com/
Games Unlimited (US): http://www.bgamers.com/gamesunl.htm
The Domino Guy (US): http://thedominoguy.com/
Spirit Games (UK): http://www.spiritgames.co.uk/
FIDO (Germany): http://www.dominospiel.de/fido/index.htm 

You'll find more links and other stuff on my domino page:

of it).
