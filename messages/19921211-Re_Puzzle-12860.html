Is this the right group?  (You may consider that as part of the puzzle

No, this is *not* the right group. This group is meant to deal with perfect
information, pure strategy games. These things are puzzles, not games; the
whole point of them is that you *don't* have perfect information; and they
are much more tests of deduction and lateral thinking than of strategy.

The group you want is "rec.puzzles". As a bonus (:-), I'll give you a free
hint: look up these puzzles in the rec.puzzles FAQ list *before* you post
them. Most of these situation puzzles have been posted there several times
already, and people tend to get a bit peevish about the amount of
repetition...
