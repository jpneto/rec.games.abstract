http://chessex.com/mats/Battlemats_&_Megamats.htm

I recommend you go to the "factory second" link there, especially if you will
be cutting the mat up into the desired board shape. A Battlemat with 1" hexes
can be cut into a hexagonal shape of (at least) 8 hexes on each side. If you
have a sewing machine, you could zigzag stitch the edges with colored thread
to label them and prevent any possible fraying.

If you're also looking for tokens, Go stones are at
