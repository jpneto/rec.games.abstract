Now place semisphere pieces along opposite sides.  The pieces being in four
different sizes and four different colors, four players can play at once!

player has one of the smallest pieces with a 'T' in it and is trying to get
their 'T' to the low point opposite them, or capture the other persons 'T'.

smaller size.  You can move to any open postion on the same terrace that does
not require jumping over an oponents piece.  You can move up on space diagonally
