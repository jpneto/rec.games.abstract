You get a lot of extra insight in one specific initial position
because it gets investigated frequently and thoroughly. Such depth, in
absolute terms, can not easily be developed if there's an array of

an array of opening positions too. And their diversity doen't prevent
you from "exploring the depth of the game".

The discussion is rooted in what you would call "the game". If "Chess-
as-is" is meant, yes then other initial positions will prevent you
from exploring the depth of "the game".
