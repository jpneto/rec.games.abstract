There's a mistake in article 377. The correct formula for Pn:

                       (N-1)!            k1  k2         k/n-1/                kn
  Pn =   SUM      ___________________  P1  P2  ...P/n-1/      (1-P1-P2-...-Pn)
        k1>=2         
        k2>=2       k1!k2!...kn!
        ...
      k/n-1/>=2
 kn=N-1-k1-...-k/n-1/>=0   

              N-1          N-1             N-2
E.g. P2=(1-P2)   -(1-P1-P2)   -nP1(1-P1-P2)      /after simplifying the formula above/
Other argue: The first term means: nobody chose number 2 /only me/
                      second term: nobody chose 1, either /bad for me/
                       third term: only one player chose 1 /bad, too/
