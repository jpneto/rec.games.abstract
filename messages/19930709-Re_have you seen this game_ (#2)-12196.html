I believe I have seen the game of which you spoke.  I will try to track down
the rules.  But, in the meantime, I believe it is a hexagonal variant of Trax. 
The object is to create a loop of your color while preventing your opponent(s)
from creating a loop of theirs.  You begin the game by each drawing N tiles
from the bag.  You then take turns placing one of your times on "board" and
then drawing a replacement tile.  First player to create a loop of his/her
color wins.  I THINK!
