The game consists of a woorden tiangle with holes in it :



               o   o   o   o   o  
                 o   o   o   o
                   o   o   o
                     o   o
                       o



and some pins: 5 x white
              4 x red
              3 x blue
              2 x green


The colored pins are interesting, but it could be a form of triangle
solitaire.  The pins "capture" as in checkers by jumping over a pin
immediately in front of it into an empty space.  So with an opening set up of:

		* * * * *                                  * = pin
		 * * * *                                   o = empty space
		  * * *
		   * *
		    o

There are two possible moves (miror images of each other):

		* * * * *     and	* * * * *
		 * * * *		 * * * *
		  o * *			  * * o
		   o *			   * o
		    *			    *

The object is to continue such captures until only one pin is left.
The only thing that worries me is that I've never seen a set with
different colored pins.  My only other suggestion is that it could be a
variation of Nim (which, I believe, has been mentioned on this group
before, so I won't describe it now).

I hope this helps.  I would be interested in seeing what other answers you
receive.
