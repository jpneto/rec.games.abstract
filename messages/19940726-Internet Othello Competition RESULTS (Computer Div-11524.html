The Computer division of the second Internet Othello Competition has
just completed, with results listed below.  Congratulations to Michael
Buro on another convincing win for his program, Logistello!  The human
divisions continue ... results will be posted when available.

Seven strong programs entered, and played a double-round-robin tournament
to determine the eventual standings.  All games were played on the Internet
Othello Server.  The third Internet Othello Competition will begin in early
to mid September ... send me e-mail if you have a program which you'd like
to enter or if you'd like to enter yourself.  For complete round-by-round
results, log on to IOS (telnet faust.uni-paderborn.de 5000) and type
"tour res mail".
