This is a nice game.  I played the Axiom program several times and I
like the general feel.  It took me two games to realize that
interfering with your own towers is just as bad as allowing your
opponent to collapse your towers.  I like the balance between starting
new towers on the board and strenthening existing towers.  My only
criticism is that the long-range influence of pieces seems to be
minimal and thus strategizing seems difficult; however this is not a
big problem because of the relatively small board size you have
chosen. From a tactical standpoint this game is interesting and a lot
of fun.  Good work!
