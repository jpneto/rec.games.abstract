DEMOCRACY RESTORED TO TABULA NOMIC

After the recent hiatus caused by a bloodless coup by a group calling
themselves "The Triumvirate", the dictatorship at Tabula (a six month
old game of Nomic) has ended, and democratic processes have been
restored, as promised by the evildoers.  With peaceful and legal
process back, Tabula is now seeking insightful and talented
individuals to join in their great democratic experiment.

Information on Tabula, including the current rules, can be found via
the web at http://www.cse.ogi.edu/~trost/nomic/reed/ .  People
interested in joining or watching can send mail to
nomic-request@reed.edu.
