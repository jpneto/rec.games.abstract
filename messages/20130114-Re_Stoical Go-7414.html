In that thread, I also mentioned a few alternative rules resulting in hard-finite games (http://www.lifein19x19.com/forum/viewtopic.php?p=111505#p111505):

a) If your opponent has captured n stones on his turn, you can't make a capture on your next n turns. Further stones captured by the opponent are added to n.

b) If your opponent has captured n stones on his turn, neither player can make a capture until the total number of non-capturing moves played afterwards equals n.

c) If you capture n stones on your turn, you can't make a capture again on your next n turns.

These rules would change the game completely, but they could be interesting experiments anyway. I suspect the first one would give far too much of an incentive to make captures, but the second one should be more balanced.
