I'll try to help you word it in english for the layman :)

Setup: 
On a 6x6 board write the numbers 1-6 six times randomly.  One number in each square.
The game is scalable so if it were an 8x8 board then one would use the numbers 1-8 etc...
In other words: on an n x n board randomly designate each number 1 - n to each square n times.

Play:
Mark any number somehow (we circled them).  The next player then scratches out numbers adjacent to the marked number and/or other numbers scratched out during that turn.

If a player has no legal move for either part of their turn (marking or scratching) they lose.

Note on tactics:
if someone circles a 4 or higher one unscratched or marked space away from the edge they risk instantly losing.
if someone circles a 2 or higher one unscratched or marked space diagonally adjacent to a corner square they also risk instantly losing.
