I just dug out my 20+ year old copy.  It helps a lot if you bear in
mind that the game was intended as an adjunct to secondary school
study of basic logic; it is basically a programmed learning text with
a game sort of attached.  I cannot imagine the whole thing being
taught to a bunch of game-playing friends in a single session the way
you teach, say, 1830 or whatever.  Even if your buddies are all facile
with first-order predicate calculus -- isn't everyone? -- the Polish
prefix notation will probably be unfamiliar.

It is also a charming example of what low-budget typesetting looked
like in the days Before Macintoshes Stalked The Earth...
