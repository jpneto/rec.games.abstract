** STOICAL GO **

In this Go variant, standard ko rules don't apply. Instead, it's illegal to make a capture if your opponent made a capture on his previous move.

All other rules are the same as in Go. Suicide of one or more stones is not allowed, and area scoring is used.

All known forced Go cycles (most of which are described at http://senseis.xmp.net/?Cycle) are impossible with this rule. The nature of the rule itself suggests that forced cycles are either impossible or astronomically rarer than they are in Go when the superko rule is not used.
