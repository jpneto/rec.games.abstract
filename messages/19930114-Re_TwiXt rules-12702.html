Okay, I apologize for not defining my terms very well. By "intended link" I
meant "two pegs of your color that are the correct linking distance apart."
Clearly in your example there are no such pair of pegs. Of course you may not
link if you don't have two pegs of your color that are a knight's move apart.
My intention was not to clarify what constitutes a drawn position, but to
clarify what constitutes a legal link.
