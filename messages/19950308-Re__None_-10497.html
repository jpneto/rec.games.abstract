Same type of problem: at any *intersection* of lines, you have to leave it
right after entering.  The only way to trace an odd number of paths from an intersection is if you start or end the trace at that intersection.  This 
means there can be a maximum of two points with an odd number of paths leading
from it.  Since the intersections at the middle of each side of the rectangle
each have weight=5, the diagram is untraceable.  A simple modification of the
diagram to make it feasible is to remove the middle line marked <---.
