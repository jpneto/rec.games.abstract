Thank you for the feedback, Hazen.  Certainly the first player to obtain a 6-6-6 is more likely to win.  Even the 5-5-5.  Or a 6-6-5-5.  As in all games, once you've attained an advantage of some sort, you're more likely to win.  What matters is that Player 1 is not more likely to get the first 6-6-6 just because he's the first to roll.  The reason is that Player 1 makes a half roll of 6 dice on his first turn, and Player 2 makes a full roll of 12 dice on his first turn.  And then both players make full rolls of 12 dice on every turn thereafter.
