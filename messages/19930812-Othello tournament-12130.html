The British Challengers' Othello Tournament.

This Othello tournament will take place in Sheffield, England,
at the Charnwood Hotel, 10 Sharrow Lane, Sheffield on Sunday 22nd 
August 1993. The tournament is open to ALL humans who have not qualified
to play in the 1993 British Othello Championships (which will be
held at the same venue on Saturday 21st and Sunday 22nd August).
Beginners  are especially welcome.

The highest placed British competitor will automatically qualify for
next year's British Championships.

Further information is available from Robert Stanton
(R.Stanton@sheffield.ac.uk), or (to a lesser extent) from myself.
