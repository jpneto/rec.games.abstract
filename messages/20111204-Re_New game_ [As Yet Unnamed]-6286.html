I have some doubts about the "usually". The object is to leave

obviously met, but the reverse is not true. Here's where the 'approach

here, so you can't leave the 'rounding up' to the players. Hence the
condition that not only enforces it, but fits in perfectly with the
misere object.
And I can envision lots of positions where the winning player has more
than one unit.
