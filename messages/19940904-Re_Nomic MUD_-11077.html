most of the extant games of nomic on the net are conducted by email.
i've seen hints of one or two being conducted via world wide web.

if i had a site i could theoretically put up an interactive game (it
wouldn't quite be a mud, as using a full-blown mud driver to support
nomic is probably overkill).  unfortunately, none of the
internet-accessible sites i have access to would allow me to run a
mud.

i am considering starting up another email nomic-type game, with a
initial ruleset similar to, but significantly different from, that
used by agora.  if you're interested, send me email. :)
