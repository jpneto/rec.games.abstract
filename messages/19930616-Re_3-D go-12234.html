I find the idea of a random lattice very appealing.  It would provide for
very unusual strategy.  The obvious point about 3-D go, is that a tetrahedral
lattice is both regular and gives each point four neighbors.  I haven't seen
anyone raise this possibility yet.  As for what it would do to the strategy,
who knows?  I'd like to give it a try if anyone comes up with a way. 

The random lattice doesn't require three dimensions.  You can generate the
same network on two dimensions, you'll just have a lot more crossing lines
if you draw it out.
