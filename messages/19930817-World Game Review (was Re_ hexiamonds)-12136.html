I would recommend World Game Review #10 to anyone who has ever been
interested in chess variants; it includes desciptions of some 677
variants, of which I estimate 250-350 are described in enough detail to
play. (Many of them are too complex to describe in the space he gives,
including such major games as Shogi, Xiang Qi, and Ultima; but the ones he
does describe are great, including many of the most popular PBM chess
variants.)
