Here are the rules for the game 'Duell'.

Board:  9x8 squares

Pieces:
Nine white and nine red dice, each side having
one king die showing ones (represented as '+') on all sides,
and eight dice showing points as usual, all with similar orientation.

Initial setup (upper side only/dice unfolded):

               3   3   3   3   +   3   3   3   3
5126+6215     651 512 126 265 +++ 265 126 512 651
.........      4   4   4   4   +   4   4   4   4
.........
.........
.........
.........
.........      4   4   4   4   +   4   4   4   4
5126+6215     156 215 621 562 +++ 562 621 215 156
               3   3   3   3   +   3   3   3   3

Moves:
The number of squares a die is moved in one turn is exactly
the number the die shows on its upper side before the move.
The dice are not shifted, but toppled over between squares.
The direction may be changed at a right angle at most once in a turn.
A die may not move over squares occupied by other dice.
If a move ends on a square occupied by an opponent's die,
that die is removed (just like in chess).

Winning goals:
Hitting opponent's king die or
moving own king on opponent's king starting square.

An example shows it all:

5126+6215 5126+6215 5126+6215 5126+6215 5126+6215 5126+6215 5126+6215
......... ......... ......... ......... ......... ......... ....x....
......... ......... ......... ......... ......... .....3... ....2<...
......... ......... ......... ......... .....6... .....^... .....^...
......... ......... ......... .....4... .....^... .....^... .....^...
......... ......... .....1... .....^... .....^... .....^... .....^...
......... .....3... .....^... .....^... .....^... .....^... .....^...
5126+6215 5126+^215 5126+^215 5126+^215 5126+^215 5126+^215 5126+^215

The die showing a 6 is moved 5 squares forward and one to the left,
ending with a 2 on the upper side, threatening to hit the king die
and thus winning the game.
There are three possible defense moves: one of the dice with '5'
may move to the square marked 'x' to block the way to the king,
or the king may move to that field, now only one square from the
attacking die.
