Nim is so classic, that the game in it's pure form (perhaps) ought not
be discussed further on the newsgroup.  So, I won't spoil anyone's fun
by giving away the solution.  But a GIANT hint is (if you want to read
it --- you may want to just skip to the next paragraph --- last
chance) is look at the binary expansion of sizes of the piles (rows).

Try small examples of this game, with 3 piles, and see what you can
come up with.  (The solution generalizes to more piles.)

If you're stumpted and curious, many refrences give it away, including
Winning Ways Vol I (Berlekamp, Conway, Guy)
