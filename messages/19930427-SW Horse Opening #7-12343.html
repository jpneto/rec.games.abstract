Red 56 cannon central Horse vs Black R1+1 variation

In my last posting, Black's R9+1 variation did not cope too well with Red's
56 cannon and central Horse. In this post, Black's R1+1 variation will be
described.

Black has several options:

Option 1
7  ...   R4+5
8  R2+6  H7+6
9  R24   H6+7
10 C5+4! H3+5
11 R8+7  Black loses a piece

Option 2
7  ...   A6+5
8  S7+1  R4+5
9  R2+6  H7+6
10 R24   H6+7
11 C5+4  H3+5
12 R8+7  E7+5 (If R43?; R45 R3+1; R84 Red wins a piece)
13 R8-5  H5-3
14 H7+8  Red may gain more soldiers in the endgame

Option 3
7  ...   R98
8  R2+9  H7-8
9  S7+1  R4+3
10 R8+6  H8+7
11 R87   E7+5

Black is down by a solider but has more development in its left wing. Hence
Black reaches a satisfactory position in this option.

Conclusions: Black R98 option of exchanging Rook with Red is the best of 3
options. It prevents Red R2+6 and winning the central Black soldier.
