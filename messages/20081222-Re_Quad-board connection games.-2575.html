A simple solution, OC, is to just mark out e-even stretches of border,
alternately coloured to each player, and then just play Atoll on that.

zones, and play Y on that.

In all cases, the over-riding simple joint goal, applicable to all
cases, is...

a)  join two opposite sides, or

In the Y-like games, (a) can be interpreted in a conforming way.
