I've been playing a couple of Yinsh games, and I found out all my 
opponents were keen on the corners/edges. I'm not convinced about that 
stratey so stuborn me tried to keep my rings as mobile as possible (in 
the boards center), in stead of going to the edges. I won most of the 
games, but that doesn't say much because my Yinsh-skill is poor :-) I'de 
like to hear others' thoughts about the centre/border positions.

When I move my rings, I usually move it to a place where I would like to 
have a disc. But I also noticed that the rings are excellent for blocking 
your opponent and limit his options (I know, it's obvious, but sometimes 
it's good to think about the obvious things :-).
