What is Mornington Crescent?


Players take turns naming London subway stops.  The first person to name
Mornington Crescent wins.  Of course, you can't say it too soon or it's no fun
to play with you . . .


This is an extremely simplivied version of the game, though...
moves are determined by a very strong aesthetic sense of what
is a "good move"; while these aren't rules, just conventions,
they are universally understood and a good player must work within
them to gain any respect.  See the group alt.games.mornington.cresent
(yes, spelled wrong) for some excellent playing.
