variation)

9   ...    C6+4! 
10  E3+1  the right choice; If R3+1? C67; R34  C7+3; A4+5  C79; G54 C2+2!
Black sacrifice left Horse in exchange for attack on Red's right wing.

10  ...    C67
11  R34    R8+4
12  A4+5   A4+5

Black has several options such as S3+1 or C75 in its counter attack plan.
Hence the position is even.
