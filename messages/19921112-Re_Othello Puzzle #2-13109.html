The idea is to force black to sooner or later play b2. This can be
done by limiting his moves. A good move for white is h6:

  a b c d e f g h
1 . o o o o o o .
2 o . o o o o . .
3 o o x o o o . .
4 o o o o x o . x
5 o o o o x x o .
6 o o x x x x . o
7 . . x . . . . .
8 . . . . . . . . 

I'll not go through a detailed analysis of the rest of the game, but I
believe that black will soon be forced to play g2, yielding th corner
to white and then shortly afterwards black will have to play b2.
An alternative move for white could be h5. It gives one extra move for
black, but I think it will also force black to move g2 and later b2.
