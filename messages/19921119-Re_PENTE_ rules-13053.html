	Pente is played on a 19x19 grid (a go board) with players taking black


Actually, I have read that a Pente board is only 15x15.


No, it's definitely 19x19 (I have one).  There are some graphic designs 
around the edges which seem to cut into the playing space, but according 
to the rules it is legal to play on top of these designs as though they 
weren't there.  Thus all 361 points are available to play.  In practice 
I've never played or seen a game that got anywhere near those outer 
boundaries, as play gravitates naturally around the first stone played, 
which is required to be in the center.  The board might as well be 
infinite, IMO.
