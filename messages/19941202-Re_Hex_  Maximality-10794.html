to the end of the tree (with help from me) can always win playing
first on the 7x7 board.  (Actually it knows three ways to win,
starting moves d4, c5, and b6).  Jing Yang has actually written
down a fairly straightforward complete solution with first move d4;
one can play automatically by following his patterns, and he has
a program which does so.  You can probably write to him care of
