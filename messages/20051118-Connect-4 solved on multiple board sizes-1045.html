I just completed solving connect-4 on all board sizes up to width+height=15.
From the perspective of the first player, the perfect play outcomes are:

height

10     =     =

9     =     =     +

8     =     =     -     +

7     =     =     +     =     +

6     =     =     -     +     -     -

5     =     =     =     =     +     +     +

4     =     =     -     =     -     -     -

    4     5     6     7     8     9     10     width

This table, source code, a perfect playing applet, and some connect-4 links
are available at http://www.cwi.nl/~tromp/c4/c4.html

Next on the todo list is solving the 8x8 case, which may take a few years...
