You can play with as many rows as you like (well, at least 3
really). Either getting or not getting the last match are
playable. Well, I say playable, but the only problem is the game is
very easy once you know the correct strategy (which is easy to
describe). I can tell you if you want, but it might spoil your fun! If
you want a reference, try
  Berlekamp, Conway and Guy     Winning Ways for your Mathematical Plays
