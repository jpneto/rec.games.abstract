I have received several requests in the last few months for copies
of my papers on Connect-Four and Go-Moku.

As of today, these are available through anonymous ftp at the following
address:

		ftp.cs.vu.nl

		victor/connect4.ps.Z
		victor/gomoku.ps.Z

The Connect-Four paper is my M.Sc. thesis, written in 1988.
It is a 91 page document describing all the techniques used to
solve the game.

The Go-Moku paper is technical report CS93-02 of the University of Limburg,
written in 1993.
It is a 12 page document describing in general terms how go-moku is solved,
with a focus on threat-space search.

A more extensive description of the solution of Go-Moku will be available
in two/three months, when I have finished my Ph.D thesis.

Victor Allis.
