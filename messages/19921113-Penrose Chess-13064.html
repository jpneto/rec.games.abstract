We define a non-move called Expansion: 
Players A and B take turns laying down 64 Penrose tiles according to
the established Penrose rules.

White draws a random tile from a bag places it on the table. Expansion
occurs.  Players place their chess pieces as far from each other as they
can. They start to play chess [modified rules would need to be made.]

If current_player says: "There's not enough room on the board for me
to make my next move" then one expansion occurs. current_player must move.
