The books by Tom Braunlich are cleverley titled _Pente Strategy I_, _Pente
Strategy II_, and _Pente Strategy_, which pretty much contains the text of
the first two books under one cover.  The last book is 132 pages,
paperback, published by Warner Books, ISBN 0-446-38231-1 (USA), or
0-446-3823-X (Canada).  Dunno if that helps any for those of you in
Australia. :-) If you find PSII but not PSI, don't worry; PSI was fairly
rudimentary, so you can probably follow PSII without too much trouble.
