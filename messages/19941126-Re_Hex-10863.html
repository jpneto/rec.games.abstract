hde@cs.cmu.edu (Herbert Enderton) writes:


Determination of the set of winning moves for 7x7 is probably too hard
for me.


I seem to recall, though, that you have solved 7x7 (presumably
a win from the central position).  Is this correct?  Do you
have results for 8x8 or 9x9?


Yes, moving in the center wins in 7x7.  Jing Yang wrote out a
proof with a complete strategy; it came to 20 pages or so. 
The 2-2 and 3-3 points (B6 and C5) on the short diagonal win too.
My program can play perfectly starting from any of those three moves.
Probably there are many other winning moves.  It's MUCH harder
o evaluate a borderline move (e.g. A4) then a strong move like those.
I haven't tried 8x8 or 9x9 yet.  I wonder if on larger boards
there are more or fewer winning first moves.
