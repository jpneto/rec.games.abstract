This is close to "mobility optimization".  The general idea is to
increase your available moves while decreasing your opponent's
available moves.  In the first half of the game, trying to get as few
pieces as you can approximates this.

David Levy's _Computer Games_ has a decent section on Othello
algorithms.  (I forget whether it was volume 1 or volume 2).

positions.
