The following are the results and some transcripts from the 1992 CSC Waterloo
Computer Othello Open, held at the University of Waterloo in late November
last year.  A record 25 computer programs and one human participated, with
entries from many parts of the world.  Competition was fierce, and the
5 rounds allotted failed to clearly establish a winner.  The final rankings
(with ties broken by disc differential) are given below:

What follows are a selection of the transcripts from this tournament,
in no particular order.  In each case, the first program listed was
black (hence played first).  To play through the games, set up the
board in the initial configuration, and play the moves in order
1, 2, 3 ... as indicated from the grid.

If there's interest, I can post analyses of some of the better games
here.  Let me know.
