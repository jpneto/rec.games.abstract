RULES FOR GAME OF LIFE:

1: pieces are placed on a rectangular grid
2: a grid location's neighbors are all eight of the adjacent locations
3: each iteration, a list of doomed stones and a list of new stones are
   maintained.
4: an empty space with exactly three neighbors is added to the NEW list
5: a stone with <2 or >3 neighbors is added to the DOOMED list

6: after all the locations are checked stones are put in the spots
   on the NEW list and removed from spots on the DOOMED list.


7: Repeat until bored (should take about a week.)
