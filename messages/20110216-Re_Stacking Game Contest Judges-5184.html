Each of 3 judges has 100 points to work with


Or, a more natural system, the judges can give each game any score between 0
and 100.  They don't all have to add up to 100.  But then after their scores
are submitted, normalize the data so it's as though the judges only had a
total of 100 points to distribute to the games.

E.g. If a judge gives the six games (20, 30, 50, 70, 80, 100), totalling
350, divide each of those scores by 3.5, to get (5.7, 10.6, 14.2, 20, 22.9,
28.6) totalling 100.
