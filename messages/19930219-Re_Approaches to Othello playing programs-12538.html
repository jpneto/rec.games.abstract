Probably the reason was that the amount of computation necessary for
doing lookahead is roughly proportional to the square root of the
average branching factor.

For more detailed analysis I suggest:

"An Analysis of Alpha-Beta Pruning" by Donald E. Knuth and Ronald W. Moore
	in 'Artificial Intelligence' 6(1975), pp 293-326
