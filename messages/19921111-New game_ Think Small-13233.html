In honour of the creation of the rec.games.abstract newsgroup, this is an
announcement of a strange game to confound and confuse. Everyone is encouraged
to enter, and the rules are as follows:
	
	(1) Think of any whole number greater than or equal to 1.
	(2) Mail me with that number as the Subject line of your message.
	(3) After a certain date and time, whoever has mailed me the lowest
	    number THAT NO-ONE ELSE HAS SENT wins (and gets a small and
	    insignificant prize).
	(4) You may not enter more than once.
	
Notes:
	(a) Mail will be processed automatically, so the subject line
	    must consist *only* of digits in the range 0 to 9.
	(b) Closing date is 12 noon GMT on Friday 13th November 1992.

That's it.  Good luck,
