I have the game at home, not at my office ;-)
so I'll have a look at the rules this evening;
(you know I haven't played it for a long long time).

Rules are roughly as follow:

 - each camp have nine dice: 8 ordinary dice and a special one ("all ace");
 - each army initially stands on the first line  of  its side, 
   with the king in the center (or was this the cardinal ?); 
   what I can't remember by now is the disposition of the armies...
 - at his turn, each player moves one of his dice by the number indicated
   on it; the king only moves by 1 place;
 - when a move ends on an enemy dice, this one is removed from the board;
 - one player wins when he captures the enemy king.
