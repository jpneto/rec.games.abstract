This doesn't work. Black will simply play optimally from start and play move 5 as Black to retain his advantage.

Giving White the option to pass a number of times, as a komi of sorts, seems like the best way to balance the game, although a bit too coarse-grained, since the optimal komi would be very low.



Quite right, what a boneheaded suggestion of mine. Yes, it would have to be after 3 or 5 moves - 5 would be interesting I think, as it's likely to constitute the first approach move, which makes strategic understanding particularly important.

I think the komi / pass idea is a good one, but I suspect it will be quite hard to balance without a considerable number of games to draw upon. Bill seems to think 3 is about right, but I'm unconvinced - not that I have good reason to think otherwise, but it just feels too arbitrary to me.
