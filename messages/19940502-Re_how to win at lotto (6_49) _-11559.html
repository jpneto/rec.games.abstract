If you know what other people are betting then you can improve your
payoff by playing numbers that no one else plays.

I have heard that numbers like 1,2,3,4,5,6 (6 number lotto) get played
so much that if it ever came up each winner would get $20.  Also
numbers that translate into 3 dates (3 of 1-12 and 3 of 1-31) also
generate low payouts if they win.

Get a record of the number of winners and number of winners (including 0)
for your lottery.

(Remember that the average payout for these lotteries is $.50 per $1.00 bet.
You cannot count on it to make money.  You can hope to get very lucky.)
