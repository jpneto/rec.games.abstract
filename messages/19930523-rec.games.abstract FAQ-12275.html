3. Anonymous FTP archive

	An archive of items related to abstract games is maintained at
	pprg.eece.unm.edu [129.24.24.10], in /pub/rec.games.abstract
	This directory is organised as follows:

		bibliography	books, papers, magazines related to abstract
				games.

		games/		contains rules and discussion of various
				abstract games: footsteps, sprouts, pente,
				shogi, xiangqi, trax, twixt.

		programs/	computer players for games.

	Rules and other items for additional games are welcomed.
	Send submissions for the archive to neilb@chama.eece.unm.edu
