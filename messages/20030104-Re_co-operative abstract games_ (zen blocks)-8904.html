I don't know. I do have one game that can be played entirely competitively
but, if two or more players cooperate, they can raise their score. The
target is still to have the highest individual score but cooperation can
pay off.
NB This isn't a sort of forming alliances and has the downside that those
that try to cooperate but fail can fail badly.
In all other respects it's as competitive as any form of six-sided ludo
could be!
