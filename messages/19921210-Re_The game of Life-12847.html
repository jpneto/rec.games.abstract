simulation thing.  i dont recall much of the specifics, and that's why i am
posting this.  it was something like a piece could stay alive if it was
adjacent to 2 or three other pieces, and there were rules for new pieces

1) A living cell with exactly one or zero living neighbors dies of
  isolation.



2) A living cell with four or more living neighbors dies of overcrowding.



3) An empty cell with exactly three living neighbors becomes alive.
