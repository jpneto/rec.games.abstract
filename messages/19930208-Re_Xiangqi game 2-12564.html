: 1.  Che3   Cbe8
A very old opening variation indeed. More 'modern' variations of the
contra-cannon are:  
  1.  Che3  Hg8
  2.  Hg3   Rh10
  3.  Rh1   Ch4
  4.  g5    Cbe8
The above variation is more 'flexible' as Black can always select
other openings at move #3.
Many books have been written on this contra-cannon opening.

Red has won the game by move 13 due to sloppy play by Black. Perhaps
Xiangqi beginners should concentrate their opening just by using solely
the central cannon opening and ignoring other more subtle openings such as the
Elephant opening which requires very good middle and end-game skills.
