Quick tip regarding how to play abstract games by Email:  Make a
Google Presentation in Google Documents consisting of a board and
pieces, and then share it with your opponent using Google's sharing
functionality.  It's very simple and you can make the graphics as nice
as you want.  Truly, this is waaay better than using ascii (I'm
looking at you Bill Taylor).
