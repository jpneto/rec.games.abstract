Agreed.  In the case of Basic though, I accidentally happened upon a
game that was unimaginably simple, way beyond my expectations.  It
more than just satisfies a constraint.  It turns the constraint on its
end.  I couldn't have purposely designed such a simple game.
Seriously, it had to happen by accident.  When you have a game that
simple, its game tree only matters if it's small or flawed.  There's
nothing you could do to Basic that would make it any more basic,
without destroying it in the process.
