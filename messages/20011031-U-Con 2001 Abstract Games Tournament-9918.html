Howdy everyone! I'm planning to host a small (up to 16 participants) abstract
games tournament at the U-Con gaming convention to be held in Ann Arbor,
Michigan on November 16-18, 2001. The tournament itself will take place
Saturday from 3pm until 7pm EST, and the entry fee is US$3. Here's a copy of
the text from the program:

Participants will compete in a series of 2-player abstract games. "Abstract"
means no random elements and no hidden information (e.g. Checkers). To
emphasize original thinking over prior study, participants won't know which
games they will be playing beforehand. Clocks optional (BYO). Tournament
structure and prizes will depend upon participation.

Registration and more information can be found at the U-Con web site:

www.deathstar.org/~ucon

I hope to meet some of you there. If it goes well, maybe this will become an
annual event at U-Con.
