I find it hard to believe that no one has thought about it before, but after checking various sources (Wikipedia article about connection games and Y in particular, iggamecenter and others, I cannot find any reference to this game so here it goes. The game is played on a metahex board (a board in the form of a hex consisting of hex cells, like the one used in Havannah). Consecutive pairs of edges are marked with a distinct color and called sides. Corners where the sides meet belong to both sides simultaneously. The goal of the game is to create a chain of stones linking all three sides (like in Y).
