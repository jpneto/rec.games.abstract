There's a 100% markup at the store. So if they sell it for $40 you'll sell it for $20.  Let's say the thick, juicy pieces are 75% of the cost and that they cost you $1 each in a small production run.  That's 15 pieces.  Total..  Not enough for a decent game.  The only way to lower your costs is to produce thousands of games in a single production run and then liquidate them for 1/10 what you paid when nobody wants it.  

Have fun [shaking head, sighing heavily]
