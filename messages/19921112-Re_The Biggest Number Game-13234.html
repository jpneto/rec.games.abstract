As you might well imagine, this sort of problem never came up with my six year 
old brother! 

A number specification rule could be added along the lines of this: the number
you specify must be in terms of "descriptive" digits, ie. you could say "My
number begins with 45 9's, and then ends with 15 million 0's", but you couldn't
say "My number is (10!)!" since that doesn't describe the digits explicitly.

Perhaps the best way (only way?) is to play it with a six-year old!
