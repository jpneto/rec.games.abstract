I have a game. Any number of people can play, taking turns. Each turn, a
player says something (anything at all). The winner is the first to say
'I win'. This player then gets a number of points equal to the number of
turns played in that round. You can keep playing until everyone gets
bored. Then you add up your points, and the highest score wins the game.
Is this an interesting game?
