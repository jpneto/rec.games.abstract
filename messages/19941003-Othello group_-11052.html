I went back and looked at the Summer '94 *Othello Quarterly*, which

play a game, I got cut off three-quarters of the way through!

There's a listing of e-mail addresses of Othello players in the Summer 
and Fall '94 issues of *OQ*, by the way. I don't know how many do or 
don't read this group.
