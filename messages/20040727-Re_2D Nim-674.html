In fact I misread your original post on this as follows - that in the build-up
ANY polyomino can be added (as long as it has at least one edge in contact).

BTW - I really *love* your idea of using a build-up phase as the preferred

sorts of games as well?  It is not unlike the "adoption" equalizer
of Unlur and similar games.   Very high-tension!

Do you have "Winning Ways"? All four volumes? 


Yes, though it's two volumes for me, each having 2 divisions.

In fact I have a very special copy.  A late colleague of mine visited Conway 
in Cambridge, and after a chat offered to buy them, as he had a little supply
in his office.  Conway offered to flip coins for them, my colleague to toss 
three coins. If all turned up heads he would get them for free, otherwise
he would pay 8/7 of the price (the fair amount).  My colleague accepted,
tossed, and lost, and duly paid up.  Conway signed the books for him as well.

When he died I inherited them.  So he paid extra, and I paid nothing!
[Except the life of my colleague, which I would far rather have.  :-(  ]

So I have a signed (and overpaid-for) copy of the books!   Ta-Dah!!
