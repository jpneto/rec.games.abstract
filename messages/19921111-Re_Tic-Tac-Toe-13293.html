In Winning Ways it is only proven that 9 or more in a row is a draw.

that 6 to 8 in a row is a draw. You need more complicated patterns to
prove anything for 6 to 8 in a row.
Since Go-Moku (five-in-a-row) has recently been proven by my
computer program Victoria to be a first-player win,
6 to 8 are the only three cases still to be proven.

Personally, I am convinced that each of these are a draw.
