I played the game (11x11) and won it by creating just one diagonal
(weak) connection. The game seems very interesting. It is similar to
Slither in that diagonal connections are prohibited. Similarly as in
Slither it takes a while to get a feel for what is allowed. The X
pattern serves only to avoid races, it has no other practical
application (it may have more meaning in advanced play). Konobi may
turn out to be more interesting connection game than Slither as in
Slither it is very difficult to make predictions and visualize the
effect of moves due to the "moving sands" effect. Congratulations for
another excellent game! Well done.
