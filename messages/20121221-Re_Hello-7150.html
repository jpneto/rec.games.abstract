Slither is not a game.


Slither represents an interesting solution to the square grid problem - i.e.. how to prevent draws on a square grid.  In Slither you can't place two friendly stones diagonally adjacent to each other unless there's a third friendly stone orthogonally adjacent to both of the said two.

My own solution to the square grid problem is Crossway (http://www.marksteeregames.com/Crossway_rules.pdf).  

One of my more interesting connection games is Fractal (http://www.marksteeregames.com/Fractal_rules.pdf).  In fact Fractal is probably the best and most balanced connection game available.  Or should I say not available, since it's too difficult for anyone to program.
