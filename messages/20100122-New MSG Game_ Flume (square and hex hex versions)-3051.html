New MSG Game: Flume (square and hex hex versions)

Square Flume:
  http://www.marksteeregames.com/Flume_Go_rules.pdf

Hex Hex Flume:
  http://www.marksteeregames.com/Flume_rules.html

Square Flume is an extremely simple game.  In fact it's hard to believe a
game emerges from one simple rule, but one does - a game uncannily similar
to Dots and Boxes.  Hex hex Flume is a tad more complicated with two
restrictions on stone placement.

I've always greatly admired the Dots and Boxes architecture but I never
liked its gameplay.  Now that I have Flume though I suppose it's time to
confront D&B strategy.  I've ordered a book, The Dots and Boxes Game:
Sophisticated Child's Play, as recommended to me by RWS.  I'm looking
forward to reading that.

Square Flume can be played at GWC:
