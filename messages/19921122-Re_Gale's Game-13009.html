Isn't this the game that is called Chomp in Winning Ways?


Yup.  Winning Ways also gives a related game: Given a number N,
players take turns naming divisors of N which may not be multiples of
previously named divisors.  Whoever names 1 loses.

Here's a rectangle showing the similarity between the geometric and
arithmetic game, for N = 432 = 2**4 * 3**3.
	  1   2   4   8  16
	  3   6  12  24  48
	  9  18  36  72 144
	 27  54 108 216 432
