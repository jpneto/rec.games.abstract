VOTING:
I considered the following 2 things to be important in the voting
process to encourage people to vote.
1.  You are not required to submit a game to vote.
2.  You are not required to play every game to vote.
....and 1 thing was important for receiving entries into the contest:
1.  You are not required to vote if you submit a game.

there are a slew of voting systems:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voting_system

One I thought might be interesting would be that you vote for your
favorite game and that your vote value increases for every different
game that you play.  If I play 5 games then my vote has more value
than someone who plays 1 game.

This could encourage people to play more games without losing the
people who didn't manage to play a couple of the games in the end.  If
in addition everyone gave the names of the games that they played then
we could also publish which games got played by how many voters.

In a perfect world you could only vote on a game once you beat a
certain level AI to ensure you explored it's strategic depth to the
desired extent :D
