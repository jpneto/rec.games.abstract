The notation will be:
	<pos>[-<pos>],<pos>



Where <pos> is:  <Row><Col>



The 1st position specified is pushed to the position after the comma.



You don't need the second position to specify the moving marbles
(ie  b2,b3 is fine, it implies b2-b4,b3.) Also, instead of giving
the second position, you could give the direction of the move:

  A   B
   \ /
 F--+--C    and the move b2,b3 would be specified as b2C.
   / \
  E   D
  
  
The problem here, is that the player need to know the set
of direction in addition to the positions...
