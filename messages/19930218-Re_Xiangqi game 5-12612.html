: 1.  Ege3   Che8
 Che8 should not be seen as a casual move , by all means.  
 It has been explained in Xiangqi theory that  1. ...  Cbe8 not as strong
as Che8 but probably would not make any difference except for very strong
players.


: 2.  Hg3    Hg8
: 3.  g5     Rh10
: 4.  Ch5    Cxe4+?
 Red's 4. Ch5 not as good as Rh1 but still alright here. Cxe4 is a bad
move. Though gaining a soldier, but enable Red's Horses to roam freely.
Therefore it is not a wise choice in the very early stage of the game.

I suggest a good opening to test Red's defences since he has opted to
use the Elephant opening:
