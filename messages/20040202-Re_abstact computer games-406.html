My game Zen ( http://bindweed.com/zen/zen.htm ) is a 2-player abstract

computer.  But games based on Conway's Life are not uncommon, and seem 
to be more popular among programmers than players...  If folks are 
interested, Zen is probably one of the better ones, and the computer 
opponents are decent.  (Windows only.)
