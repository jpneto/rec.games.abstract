MSO Cambridge 2002, an event with tournaments of many board and card games,
will take place at Parkside School in Cambridge UK on 4-6 May 2002.

Abstract board games in the event include Go, Chess, Xiangqi, Shogi, Oware,
Omweso, Bao, Checkers, International Draughts, Othello, Tantrix and Mamba.

More information, including an online entry form, is at
http://www.gen.cam.ac.uk/msocam02/
