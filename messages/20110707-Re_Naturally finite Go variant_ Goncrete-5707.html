My main concern is not the 'othellonian' capture but the extra 'out of

Yeah, the problem is that it's impossible to design a game as natural
as Go and which plays like Go without ending up inventing Go. If you
try to reinvent Go to get rid of the need for superko, you have two
choices: departing from Go while remaining extremely natural and
organic (as you did, purposely or not, in Sygo) or introducing some
extrinsic rule in your game while remaining closest to Go. The
Goncrete rule is the simplest and most congruent one I have found to
achieve this goal. I wonder if it could be done even better...
