The Fantasy Rules Committee was at one time one of the longest-running
nomic-like games on the net.  According to Google, the last reference to
the FRC on usenet occurred on this group in 1998
(http://makeashorterlink.com/?X1A324EF3).  So, I come here to ask: does
this still exist, or is there interest in reviving it?  I played in a
few rounds back then and would be interested to play again.

The FRC homepage is still live at
http://www.win.tue.nl/cs/fm/engels/frc/index.html, though it appears to
have stopped being updated in August of 2000.
