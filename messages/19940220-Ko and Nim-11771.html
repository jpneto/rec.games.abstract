  For those of you familiar with Berlekamp theory, here's a game
for you.  The game is just nim with 2 extra rules.  The first is 
that when a player removes counters from a pile they are placed in
a discard pile.  In addition to the normal nim moves a player may
return some or all of the discard pile to any one pile.  The second
rule is the super ko rule : a player may not make a move which 
repeats a previous position. 

  Another interesting game is where a legal move is to move part
or all of one pile onto another.  Again subject to the super ko
rule.  What happens?
