Oops --- I wasn't aware of other (similar) games like this.  I most
recently played this on Quest For Glory III, so those rules are the
ones I was using: 

When you drop beans, you include the cups on the end as part of your
move; if your last bean falls into your kalaha (?), you get one extra
move.  (You can't keep getting moves -- you get at most one extra.)

|> >Plus with the free move:
|> 
|> >        3 3 3 0 3 3
|> >      0             5
|> >        0 4 4 0 4 4
|> >        ^

Here is the 'capture' move; if your last bean falls in an empty cup,
you get your bean and the beans in the cup opposite the one you landed
in (even if the one you landed in was on the opponent's side), and put
them into your kalaha (?).


Sorry if I'm fuzzy on rules --- it's been a while since I played.  :)
