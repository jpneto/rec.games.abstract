I own Octi and like it a bunch.  I would say it does approach Chess (if
not deeper) in terms of strategic depth, because the game gives you the
ability to decide how pieces you have will move, by building them up.
Combine this with the ability for pieces to multijump, and the need to
manage resources, and you have a game with a lot depth to it.
