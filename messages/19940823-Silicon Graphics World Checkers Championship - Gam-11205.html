Here are the results for Games 7 to 10, played on Monday August 22:

Game  7: Lafferty 1/2	Chinook  1/2
Game  8: Chinook  0	Lafferty 1
Game  9: Chinook  1/2	Lafferty 1/2
Game 10: Lafferty 0	Chinook  1

Standings after 10 games (of the 20 game match):

Lafferty: 1 Win  8 Draws  1 Loss	5 Points
Chinook:  1 Win  8 Draws  1 Loss	5 Points

Game 8 was Chinook's first loss in over 140 games.  Congratulations to  
Don Lafferty for a very well played game!  Games 8 and 10 are the first
decisive results between these two contestants in 40 games.

Games 11 to 14 are scheduled for Tuesday August 23.
