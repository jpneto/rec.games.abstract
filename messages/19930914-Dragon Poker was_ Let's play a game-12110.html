  Dragon Poker(the poker varient in Robert Asprin's Myth Series) does NOT belong
in a list of games which involve 'making it up'(for lack of a better phrase) as
you go along.  You don't add rules to Dragon Poker, and you don't break them.
Nor do you add conditionals, as all of the conditional modifiers are spelled out
quite plainly in a copy of the rules for your dimension.  Dragon Poker is billed
as the most complex game in existance because it is almost impossible to know
all the conditional modifiers in effect in one game, as they are based on #,
,sex, race/genus/etc, etc of players.  Location of players, game, planet, etc.
Day, week, month, hour, etc...  Well, you get the idea.  Unfortunately, Asprin
never included a complete list of conditional modifiers for this(or any other)
dimension...

  And for Calvinball, I don't remember anywhere in the strip where Calvin or
