I think Malkav's daughter wanted to know what how the game played not
how to play the game.  In other words an evaluation of "gameplay".

First, I've seen the extra marbles (I forgot the official name.)
available wherever the game is sold.  You can also order them directly
from the manufacturer.  The three player rules are included with the
marbles.

As far as "gameplay", I think it's better.  People have noted in this
newsgroup that the games tends to reward defensive playing which can
make games drag on needlessly.  Three player games are much more
agressive because the board is more crowded.
