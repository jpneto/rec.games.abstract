Thanks for the feedback Ed. The game that Mark describes is Star as it was
described in an article in Games magazine in 1983. *Star  (note the
asterisk) is a new game descended from Star as described on the  Official
Apocryphal History  page ( http://ea.ea.home.mindspring.com/history.html )
of my web site. The scoring is similar but different. Just so reading the
History page doesn't confuse you even more, I must point out that there are
at least three ways of scoring which look very different, and two of them
played a significant role in the history.

The rules definitely need some figures as you suggest. And I notice that the
key concept of "sparks" is not discussed when the scoring is described. The
rules may be "logically" correct, but they leave something to be desired in
clarity. I will make them clearer. Thanks again for the feedback.

Ea
