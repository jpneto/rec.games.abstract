"Daniel S." <antiwork@me.com> writes:



Is it normal for a designer to suck at their own game?


It is not unusual.  When you design a game, your main focus is not how
to play well, but to make the game interesting to play, i.e., ensuring
there are no simple winning strategies and that the game is reasonably
balanced.

Also, I know game designers who hardly ever play the games they design
themselves, even if they become reasonably popular commercial games.
And if you don't play the game very much, you won't become very good.
