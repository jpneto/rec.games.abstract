BTW I discoverd two fantastic games at the International Toy Fair in
Nuremberg.

1.
Dvonn

rules at http://www.gipf.com/

I played this game 8-times (also against Kris Burm) and never lost a game.


2.
Blokus

rules and a strong robot playing this game at http://www.blokus.com/

Blokus was sold 26,000 times in France in the the last two years. It's one
of the best inventions since the discovery of Go / Weiqi. Just beautiful
and very deep! You could write a book on every piece (and there are 21
different ones). Very exciting is the start of the middle game fight (the
breakthru to the other side of the board) and the endgame. It appears the
game will soon be the national game of France.

I need to play this game every day.
