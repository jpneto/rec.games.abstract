There's an important difference.  In Byte, you move your stack closer to the
nearest stack, of either owner.  In Ayu, you move a unit closer to the
nearest *friendly* unit.  I think that would have been a problem in Byte,

stacks, creating equidistant paths.  I don't remember why that would have
caused a problem, but...   It was many years ago.
