Halma (also known as Chinese Checkers outside Germany) is a strategy
game
for three players played on a star-shaped board. Although being one of
the
classical board games, it was seldom subject to computer strategies.
We think it is time for a change!

On Thursday, September 16, 2004 we organize a Halma tournament for
computer strategies. Everybody is invited to participate!


Please contact our website at http://halma.teerun.de/
