Abstract games are not hidden information games or simultaneous move games.
They aren't Poker, they aren't Matching Pennies, and they aren't any sort of
mathematical recreation, matrix games involving two players simultaneously
choosing a row and a column respectively.

The term "abstract games" has a meaning.  Something other than games that
are abstract.  Every game is abstract.  Stones and a board with a grid drawn
on it most certainly do not preclude a game from being abstract.
