Hello everyone,

After about four years researching for a book about hnefatafl, I still haven't got a book written. I have, however, been writing a smaller scale booklet, an illustrated collection of articles, containing proposals for reconstruction of various different historical games, with the reasoning behind them. I've put a draft up on the Links section of my hnefatafl web site:

http://tafl.snigfarp.karoo.net/tafl/

It's the bottom link on the page. It's a PDF, about 1.2 megabytes in size. It's designed to be printed onto both sides of A4 paper, which can then be folded into an A5 booklet of 48 pages. The four articles I've put it in are: Reconstructing a Game for the Ballinderry Board; Reconstructing the Alea Evangelii; Reconstructing Tawlbwrdd; Reconstructing Tablut.

I hope someone finds it useful.
