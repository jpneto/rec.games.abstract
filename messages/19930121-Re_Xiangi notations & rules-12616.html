: 
: Does anybody have the exact!? rules concerning repetitive moves?
:
There are some moves which are limited to 3, like perpetually checkiung
the opponent's general; others are unrestricted eg, Rook threatens the
Horse which in turn threatens the Rook again. In such situation, a draw
can be declared if both sides do not change the moves.
