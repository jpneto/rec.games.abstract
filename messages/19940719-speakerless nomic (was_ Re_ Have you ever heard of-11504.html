In a more serious vein -- some people (including me) were sort of
exploring how one might run a "Speakerless Nomic".  It'd be worth
trying to hash out an initial Ruleset for such a beasty.

Two issues remain:

  * How are rules ordered?  The standard ruleset uses rule numbers to
    determine precedence, but without a speaker, it's not obvious how
    to number the rules.

  * Why play?  In what I'm familiar with, the "prize" for winning the
    game is becoming Speaker.  What's the prize in a speakerless
    Nomic?  Or are we back to only a single game?
