Two questions/challenges:


  1> Sprouts on surfaces of higher genus.  Can anyone even figure
out the 2 spot game on the general n-holed torus?  I haven't thought
too much about this, but naively there are infinitely many moves at
each stage.


  2>  Go with passing.  Awhile ago a go variant was discussed on
r.g.go  where a legal move was to pass and store up the move for
later use.  If you have 3 moves stored up then on your turn you can
make up to 4 moves in a row (all of which must be legal ... 2 eyes
is still enough).  How large must the board be before passing is the
correct first move?
