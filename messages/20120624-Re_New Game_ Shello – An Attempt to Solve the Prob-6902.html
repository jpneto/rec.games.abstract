Some Reddit user found a loop in Shello! It appears to require cooperation from both players, so not necessarily a killing blow, but certainly a red flag: 

"Setup: black pieces are at E1, F1, G1, H1, white pieces are at I2, I3, I4, I5, F9, G9, H9. The cycle is E1-E9, where the black piece moves from the bottom rank to the top, and captures the white pieces at F9, G9, H9. Then white plays I5-E1, capturing the black pieces at F1, G1, H1. Black plays E9-I5, capturing white on I2, I3, I4. White E1-E9, black I5-E1, white E9-I5 and the cycle is complete."
