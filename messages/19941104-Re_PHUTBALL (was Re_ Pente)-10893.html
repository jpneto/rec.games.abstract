In article <39dev1$bls@knot.queensu.ca> I made a somewhat horrible typo:


	- Jump the black "football" over a continuous serious of one
	  or more white stones vertically, horizontally, or diagonally,
	  removing the jumped stones IMMEDIATELY;   Repeat this step as
	  long as you wish (you're not forced to do multiple jumps)


A "continuous serious"?  Hmmm.

Correcting and expanding,

	- Jump the black "football" over one or more white stones,
	  which are in a straight line vertically, horizontally, or
	  diagonally, with no gaps (in other words, the football lands
	  in the first empty intersection in the direction jumped).
	  The jumped stones are removed IMMEDIATELY.  Repeat this step
	  as many times as you wish (you're not forced to do multiple
	  jumps)
