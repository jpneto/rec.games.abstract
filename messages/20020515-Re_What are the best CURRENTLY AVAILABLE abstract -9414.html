invented a new abstract game which uses a number of features I haven't
seen anywhere else, including a rapidly-shrinking playfield.  The game

Labs (www.treatmentlabs.com).  As a simple abstract game, albeit with
many variants provided, it's available for general evaluation, complete
with a printable board, all available in either an e-book or as HTML
that your browser can read.  Please try it out, look it over, and tell
me what you think.
