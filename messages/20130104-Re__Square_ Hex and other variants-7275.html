I've played the square version. It's quite good. No problems with it. I have too many opinions on these sorts of games to cover them here. 

Try reading the books: 

Mudcrack Y and Poly Y (out of print since the 70's, but I'll give you my copy for $100 :) - It's an awesome book with many weird and wonderful connection game tesselations, including the square one. 

Also, the book Connection Games, by Cameron Browne. Besides Y and Hex, the book singles out Gonnect, Star, and Conhex, as being particularly excellent. Not necessarily the ones I would take with me to a desert island, but they're certainly excellent.
