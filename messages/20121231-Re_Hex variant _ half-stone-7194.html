Here are the rules :

There is not need to change the topology board.
We change the rules and that`s it.

2 rules ;

Turns :

Turn 1 : Player 1 drops 1 half-tile
         Player 2 drops 2 half-tiles (either one the same hex or on 2 different hexes)

Turn 2 : Player 1 drops 2 half-tiles (either one the same hex or on 2 different hexes)
  
         Player 2 drops 1 half-tile

Turn 3,5,7,...2n+1 (odd) : same procedure used for turn 1 
Turn 4,6,8,...2n   (even): same procedure used for turn 2

Capturing and removing half-tiles :

In hex game it cost nothing to drop a single stone. No cost at all.
In the variant half-tile each hex with 2 different half-tiles have to be conquered.

If a player has 2 complete hexes at his colour taken in sandwich any hex with different half-tiles then this player must remove the opponent half-tile and replace it by his own colour.
There are 3 ways to take in sandwich any hex inside the board but only one way for all the hexes placed at he exterior of the board.
