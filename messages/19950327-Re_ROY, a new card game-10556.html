ROY:
====
This game is best for 2 or 3 players.



The object of the game is to be the first player to reach 100 points.



The object of each hand is to be the first player to discard all their 
cards.



1) Each player is dealt 7 cards.  The dealer then places the remainder 
of the deck face down in the middle of the table and turns the first 
card face up and places it next to the deck.  This card is the beginning 
of the discard pile.



2) Play begins with the player to the left of the dealer.



3) On each turn, a player may discard 1 or more cards.  Cards are laid 
face-up on the discard pile.  If the player cannot discard at least 1 
card, then he must draw 1 card from the overturned deck, and play 
progresses to the next player on the left.



4) On each turn, the first card played onto the discard pile must match 
either the suit or face value of the card on top of the discard pile.  
You may continue to discard more cards in the same turn according to 
these rules:



  - After your first discard, each card you play after that must
    either match the face value of the card on top of the
    discard pile, or it must be the next higher card of the same
    suit.



----------------------------------------------------------------
Example:   (H)earts  (C)lubs  (D)iamonds  (S)pades



  - You have these cards in your hand: 2S, 3S, 3C, 4C, 7H, 8H



  - When your turn comes, the card on top of the discard pile
    is the 2 of Hearts.



  - You may start by playing either a two (matching value) or
    a heart (matching suit)
