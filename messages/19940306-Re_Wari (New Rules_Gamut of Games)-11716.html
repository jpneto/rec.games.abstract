"The Glass Bead Game" (by Christaan Freiling) is in Wayne Schmittberger's
_New Rules for Classic Games_. It is a Mancala variant. "The Dominio Bead
Game", which is not, is by Sid Sackson and can be found in his legendary _A
Gamut of Games_, along with "Cups", a Mancala-like game by Arthur and Wald
Amberstone, whose work inspired "Domino Bead Game".
