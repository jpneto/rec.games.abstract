| BTW, with the name Solitaire I refer to the 1-person game played on the
| following board, where each move is a horizontal or vertical
| jump as in checkers, and the goal is to leave one peg in the center
| of the board.

This is more commonly called Peg Solitaire.  ("Solitaire", of course,
simply means any game played by a single player.)  An excellent source
of information about it, and indeed about almost any abstract game, is
_Winning Ways_, by Berlekamp, Conway, and Guy.  Chapter 23 is devoted
entirely to Peg Solitaire.
