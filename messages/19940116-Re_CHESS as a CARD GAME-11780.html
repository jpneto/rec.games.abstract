Hi.
It seems to me that it might be possible to overcome the both the problem
that card games tend to involve luck and the problem of hidden cards simply by
dividing the cards into suits before the start of the game. Give two suits to
each player. This way, as in chess, each player knows where the other is
starting from and no luck is involved in the setup. Maybe that oversimplifies
things, however. Good Luck.
