Cephalopod is my most recent and my favorite game.  Dice are used as
playing pieces.  Add a die with a pip count equal to the pip sum of
adjacent dice, and remove the adjacent dice.  High energy from
beginning to end.  No doldrums.  And no draws or ties of course.  I'm
selling this one for $6.
