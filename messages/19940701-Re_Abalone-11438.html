In <1994Jun29.115800.7984@Princeton.EDU> akgee@flagstaff.Princeton.EDU (Malkav's daughter) writes:


Does anyone out there know where I can find extra accessories and rules for
multiplayer (i.e. more than 2) Abalone?  Also, if anyone has played Abalone
with more than two players, how does it play?


The three player version is interesting to play because there are more
pieces and the initial layout makes it much harder to get into a
defense posture at the beginning. One variation that we created/liked
was that you needed at least 3 marbles of each of the other colors to
win. That way you couldn't pick on the weak person to get all 5 of
your marbles. Otherwise we tended to degenerate into a 2 on 1 battle
and the 1 always loses.
