I found copies of Jungle (also apparently known as The Animal Game) in
a small tourist junk shop in San Francisco's Chinatown. Folks might
try checking shops in similar districts in other cities. The version
I found is a very small box covered with brightly colored pictures of
the various animals depicted in the game (elephants, rats, various big
cats, etc.) with small, round, wooden pieces.

An English translation of the rules to the game can be found in
R.C. Bell's "The Board Game Book" (1983) Simon & Schuster, 
New York, NY  ISBN 0-671-06030-9.
