On 26-Jan-2010, Bill Taylor <w.taylor@math.canterbury.ac.nz> wrote:


Good game; but why the restrictions in the hex version?



Thanks Bill.  The move restrictions in hex hex Flume are an anti-meatball
strategy.  Players growing a dense meatball of stones, or a lump on the
perimeter isn't very interesting.  I wanted to put move restrictions in
square Flume as well, but they did more harm than good.  On the bright side,
square Flume ends up being a very simple game.
