  I'm the author of a new abstract strategy game mamba. I've seen a message from Sacha about the game... just few things to add, yes the game is based on alife aspects (ie the pieces are alive) and yes the concept is more similar to Go than chess. But one important thing, is that this game is a king of dynamic system to play with so after any action we bring the balance back on the board... we discover very funny tactics for the game... a kind of guerilla strategy is the better way to play for the moment.
