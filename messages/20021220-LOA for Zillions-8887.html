The current LOA for Zillions doesn't play quite correctly.
You win if and only if you make the last move and leave your
own pieces connected. If you leave your opponent's pieces
connected (with a capture), you _should_ lose, or draw if
your own pieces get connected also. Does anyone know how to
fix that in the zrf code?

I've included code for a few variants below (not hard to do,
of course). Just paste it in at the end of loa.zrf. Have fun!

J K Haugland
http://www.neutreeko.com
