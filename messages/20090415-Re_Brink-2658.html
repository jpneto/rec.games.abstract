....And here's an alternative win condition inspired by a similar idea:

4.  The player with the largest connected group on the board wins,
unless it touches the opponent's largest group.  In that case, the
player with the largest group on the board loses.

If the largest groups of both colors are of the same size, then
compare second-largest groups.  Whoever's second-largest group is
bigger wins.  If *those* are tied, compare 3rd largest groups, etc. If
it's tied all the way down the line, the game is a draw.
