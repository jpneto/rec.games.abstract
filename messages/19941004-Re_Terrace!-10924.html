Dave Kliman's article posting the rules for Terrace omitted the
victory condition.  There are two ways to win: either capture the
opponent's "T" piece, or move your own "T" piece to the corner
opposite where it started (marked "OBJ" in Dave's article but with
no other commentary given).

Dave also stated that the five types of pieces are all different
sizes.  Types A and T are the same size, and indeed a T piece can
capture A pieces (or the opposing T piece).
